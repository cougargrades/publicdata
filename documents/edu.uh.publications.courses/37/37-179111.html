ECE 6325 - State-Space Control Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECE 4375 or consent of instructor.

State-space modeling, matrix algebra, system response, coordinate transformation, stability, controllability, observability, realization, state-feedback design, observer, nonlinear system, Lyapunov functions, optimal control.
