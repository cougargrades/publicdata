MECT 6347 - Rheology Energy Related Fluids

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of the graduate faculty advisor.

The topics that will be discussed in this course include non-Newtonian fluid behavior and rheometry, which is the flow of fluids in pipe, conduits, and particulate systems; and the heat transfer characteristics of non-Newtonian fluids.
