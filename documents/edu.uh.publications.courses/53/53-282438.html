MECE 6380 - Computational Design and Optimization

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Fundamental knowledge in linear algebra, solid mechanics and finite element methods, or at the discretion of the instructor.

Optimization methods are introduced in the context of mechanical engineering. Tools for solving optimization problems are introduced using Python with SciPy. Final design challenge involves numerical optimization and physical fabrication.
Additional Fee: No
