PHAR 5207 - Herbal Medicine

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Permission of the instructor.

An elective course integrating clinical and natural therapeutics, medicinal chemistry, pharmacology, and evidence-based therapy for the advanced understanding of medicinal herbs, as well as their scientific concepts.
