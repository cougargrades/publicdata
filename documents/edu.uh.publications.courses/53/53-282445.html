PHLS 8396 - Doctoral Research

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Current enrollment as a doctoral student in the Department of Psychological, Health, and Learning Sciences. Consent of faculty advisor.

Credits for doctoral student research focused on individualized projects occurring under the supervision of a faculty member. Sample activities include attending lab meetings, collecting and analyzing data, writing peer reviewed journal articles, applying for grants, presenting research at conferences, etc. Research activities must be outside of activities required for graduation (e.g., dissertation research or paid employment (e.g., research assistantships.
Additional Fee: No
