COSC 6384 - Real-Time Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 4330 or consent of instructor.

Introduction to problems that may arise in real-time applications of digital computers; architecture of real-time systems; methods for real-time software design and implementation. (Term project required.)
