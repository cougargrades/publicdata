SOCW 6308 - Human Diversity and Human Development

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This generalist course develops knowledge and skills to apply human behavior and development theories in the context of the social environment, including how age, race, ethnicity, gender, and other variables impact behavior and development.
