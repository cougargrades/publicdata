BZAN 6356 - Advanced Database Management Tools for Business Analytics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and completion of BZAN 6354.

Course covers NoSQL and non-relational data modeling, including column family, document, graph and key-value databases and cloud technologies such as Hadoop and MapReduce.
Additional Fee: No
