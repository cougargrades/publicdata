ENGL 8340 - Early Modern English Drama

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

Critical study of playwrights and dramatic genres (tragedy, comedy, history, city comedy, tragicomedy, etc.) of early modern England (c. 1500-1650), including theatrical and performance history. Authors studied include, for example, Kyd, Webster, Beaumont, Fletcher, Cary, Dekker, Heywood, Jonson, Marlowe, Shakespeare, Ford, Middleton, Wroth. Significant engagement with critical literature. May be repeated for credit if approaches and readings differ substantially.
Additional Fee: Y
