BIOL 6357 - Community Ecology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOL 3306 and BIOL 4368 or consent of instructor.

For biology majors. Current principles and theories regarding the structure and dynamics of communities, including concepts of predation, competition, succession, diversity, and stability.
