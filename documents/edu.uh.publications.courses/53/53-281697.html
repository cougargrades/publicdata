CHIN 6350 - Studies in Chinese Cinema

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or P.B. Chinese 6350 explores Chinese film from the early to the contemporary era.

Focus is given to China’s “fifth” and “sixth” generation filmmakers, with some emphasis on early films (1930s and 1940s), revolutionary films, and selected films from Taiwan.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
