SPEC 7343 - Reading Development: From Science to Practice

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Formerly/Same as: PHLS 7343 Psychological Processes of Reading
Prerequisite: None.

Students learn the skills associated with the science of children’s reading development, focusing on understanding the cognitive, linguistic, and perceptual aspects of learning to read. Students also acquire the knowledge and skills to apply this scientific framework to create evidence-based classroom environments that foster parental engagement in their children’s educational journey.
Additional Fee: Y
