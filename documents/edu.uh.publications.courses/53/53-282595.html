BTEC 6319 - Advances in Microbial Biotechnology

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of instructor.

Advances in the genetic manipulation of microbes for industrial, agricultural, medical, and environmental applications. Students must complete a class presentation on an instructor-approved topic in microbial biotechnology.
