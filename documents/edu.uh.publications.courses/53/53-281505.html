SOCW 7371 - Trauma and Social Work Practice

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of the MSW foundation, or consent of advisor.

This course explores frameworks and skills for understanding types, history, and impact of trauma on individuals, family, and community.
Additional Fee: N
