MUSI 6251 - Advanced Performance Pedagogy

Credit Hours: 2.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in music.

Students will develop a comprehensive understanding of teaching tools, materials, and resources designed for the intermediate through advanced students as well as university students. Key topics include an overview of teaching resources, advanced technique, stylistic performance considerations, practice strategies, and the history of piano pedagogy.
Additional Fee: N
