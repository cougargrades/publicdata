CUIN 7331 - Identifying and Supporting Striving Readers

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Acceptance into the reading specialist program.

Procedures of monitoring and applying appropriate strategies, interventions, and sequential reading instruction that is based on a convergence of research evidence and that is responsive to individual student’s strengths and needs, including children whose first language is other than English, and students with reading difficulties, dyslexia, and reading disabilities.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
