PHOP 6X67 - Research Practicum A

Credit Hours: 1.0 - 6.0
Practical training in vision research methods and procedures. Provides current in-depth training on cutting-edge methods and procedures for assessment of visual optics, and psychophysical research in vision. Includes training in stimulus design and control, psychophysical methods and procedures, and psychophysical and optical image analysis.
