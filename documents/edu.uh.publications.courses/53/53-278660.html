CHIN 6364 - Issues in Chinese Language & Culture

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing, post B.A. status, or consent of the instructor.

Focus on understanding of cross-culture communications, including the interactions between the language and the culture, and analyses of the relationships between language expressions and culture perspectives.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taught in Chinese.
Additional Fee: Y
