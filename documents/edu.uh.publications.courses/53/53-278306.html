PHCA 8302 - Advanced Research Methods in Pharmaceutical Health Outcomes and Policy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHCA 7308 or consent of instructor.

To apply, conduct, and analyze classic and improved experimental, quasi-experimental, and observational study designs in pharmaceutical outcome research.
