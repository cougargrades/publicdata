MANA 7359 - Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Strategy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and MANA 7357 Fundamentals of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion.

The course focuses on organizational group level processes that impact diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI. It further examines the relationship between DEI and company strategy and performance.
Additional Fee: No
