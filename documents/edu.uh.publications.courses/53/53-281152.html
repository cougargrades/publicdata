GHL 6385 - Hall of Honor Management I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Instructor approval.

Management course emphasizing general team leadership, food & beverage management, logistics management, marketing operations management, and human resources. Management emphasis in preparation of annual Hospitality Industry Hall of Honor days.
Additional Fee: No
