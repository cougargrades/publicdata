ARCH 6320 - Materials and Assemblies

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course seeks to: -Introduce materials and assemblies in architecture, emphasizing the understanding of the comprehensive context of building technologies and culture, from the history of materials to the theory of material assemblies. -Focus on understanding materiality, structural principles, design detailing, and material behaviors. -Introduce precedents for analysis, interpretation, emulation, and design. -Cultivate a spirit of inquiry of design, construction, material usage -Establish a spirit of inquiry in material ecology.
Additional Fee: N
