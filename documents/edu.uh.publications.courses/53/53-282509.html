ECON 8335 - Macroeconometrics

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ECON 7344 and ECON 7331 or consent of instructor.

Fundamental tools from time series analysis such as the Kalman Filter and Kalman Smoother, Structural VARs, Factor Analysis, Latent Regime models, and others.
Repeatability: No

Note: Topics may vary. The course is intended for second- and third-year Ph.D. students in the Economics department.
Additional Fee: No
