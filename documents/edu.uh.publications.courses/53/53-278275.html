PHLA 7320 - Capstone

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

This course is designed to provide the students with experiences in the current discussion and regulation of human, financial, administrative and regulatory processes in health system pharmacy. The content builds on the material presented and learned in the residency and previous courses.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
