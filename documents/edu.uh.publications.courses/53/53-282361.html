ITAL 6367 - Italy’s Culture in a Transnational Context

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Italian culture and literature conceptualized not simply as the inquiry into national traditions but as the history of the interaction of cultural practices within Italy itself and in those parts of the world that have witnessed the extent of Italian mobility.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
