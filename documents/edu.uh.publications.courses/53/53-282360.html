GHL 6386 - Career Fair Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

The student will be able to identify the purpose and feasibility of a professional event. They will develop skills with managing a budget and learn the process behind the selection of dates. During the course, they will also apply creative thinking to event marketing and assess any risk associated with this event.
