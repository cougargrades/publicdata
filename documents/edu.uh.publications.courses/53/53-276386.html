CUIN 7342 - Designing Professional Development for Teachers

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course focuses on adult cognition and learning and how mathematics teachers and leaders apply adult learning theories to teacher learning and professional development. This course is also about reflecting upon and applying this understanding to your current and future role as a leader in mathematics education.
Additional Fee: Y
