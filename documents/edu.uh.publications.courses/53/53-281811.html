LAW 5101 - Health Law Research

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: N/A.

This is a specialized course on health law research. It will focus on federal and state primary legal materials related to health law, including relevant statutes, regulations, agency guidance and decisions, and case law.
Note: Course may be repeated with permission from the Law Center Office of Admissions.
