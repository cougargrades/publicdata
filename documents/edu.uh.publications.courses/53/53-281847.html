LAW 6338 - Climate Change Law

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: N/A.

This course will focus on the foundations, options and challenges to the use of environmental law to address climate change and to determine the obligations or liability of parties allegedly contributing it.
Note: Course may be repeated with permission from the Law Center Office of Admissions.
