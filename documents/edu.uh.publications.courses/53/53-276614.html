SPEC 7391 - Strategies for Collaboration, Coaching, and Consultation

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Formerly/Same as: Formerly/Same as: PHLS 7391 Collaborative Consultation and Coaching.
Prerequisite: Admission to graduate/certification program in special populations and credit for SPEC 6360.

Theories and strategies to prepare special education personnel, classroom teachers, school administrators, paraeducators, related services personnel, and other support staff for engaging in collaborative school consultation and teamwork in serving the educational needs of diverse populations are presented and practiced. Context, process, and content of school consultation are addressed.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
