LAW 5287 - Offshore Leasing

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None

the course will cover the Deepwater JOA, Participation Agreements, appropriate portions of 30 CFR, the cycle time from lease acquisition to first oil or gas, mid-stream issues (pipelines to shore and PHA’s), OCS Lease Sales, internal approval processes of Lessees and other topics.
