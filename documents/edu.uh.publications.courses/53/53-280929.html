MECT 6305 - Analytical Method in Engineering Technology

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of the graduate faculty advisor.

Applied mathematical analysis tools of ordinary and partial differential equations describing physical processes occuring in the practice of Mechanical Engineering Technology areas such as fluid dynamics, elasticity and heat transfer.
Additional Fee: Y
