MANA 7357 - Fundamentals of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

Introduces the concepts of diversity, equity and inclusion and how individuals in organizations can act with good intentions in ways that hurt others.
Additional Fee: No
