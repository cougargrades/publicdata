LAW 6210 - Construction Law

Credit Hours: 2.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

The course will provide students with an overview of the general principals of construction law and the issues construction law practitioners navigate in the representation of their construction industry clients.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
