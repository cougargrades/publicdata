CUIN 7370 - Teacher As Researcher

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission to graduate program in Curriculum and Instruction.

Examines the trend of “teachers as researchers” including both theory and practice. Covers concepts such as participant research, trustworthiness and dissemination. Guides students in action research projects.
