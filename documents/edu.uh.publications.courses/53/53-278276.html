PHLA 6221 - Advanced Health System Management

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PHCA 6321
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

This course is designed to ensure a baseline understanding of pharmacy practice, leadership and management concepts to use as a foundation through the graduate program. Students will learn how health system pharmacy operates with regard to patient care and business aspects at an introductory level.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
