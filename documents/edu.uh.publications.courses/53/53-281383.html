CUIN 8371 - Introduction to Quantitative Research

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CUIN 8370 and introductory statistics ( PHLS 7322 or equivalent); or consent of instructor.

Provides an overview of quantitative research methods with an emphasis on inquiries conducted in urban educational contexts.
