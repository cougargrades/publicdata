ENGL 8344 - Early Modern Poetry

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

Critical study of major poets, genres (lyric, epic, devotional verse, metrical Psalms, sonnet sequences, satire, pastoral, etc.), and theoretical accounts of poetry/imaginative literature of early modern England (c. 1500-1650), including authors such as Philip and Mary Sidney, Spenser, Shakespeare, Donne, Mary Wroth, Milton. Significant engagement with critical literature. May be repeated for credit if approaches and readings differ substantially.
Additional Fee: N
