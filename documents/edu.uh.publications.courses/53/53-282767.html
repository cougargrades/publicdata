MUSI 6252 - Group and Adult Piano Pedagogy

Credit Hours: 2.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in music.

Students will develop an awareness of teaching tools and materials appropriate for group piano classes designed for pre-collegiate students, adult hobby students, and university students, as well as the instruction of adult students (andragogy). Key topics include teaching resources, classroom management strategies, planning and developing curricula for group teaching and adult lessons, as well as learning theories. Activities include peer teaching and observations.
Additional Fee: N
