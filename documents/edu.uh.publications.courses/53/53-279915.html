ECE 6339 - Biophotonics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Instructor permission.

Fundamental principles of biophotonics and their applications in biological and biomedical research. Topics are light-matter interactions, optical imaging, optical and plasmonic biosensing, imaging systems and nanotechnology for biophotonics.
