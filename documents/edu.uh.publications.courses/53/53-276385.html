CUIN 7341 - Mathematics Education Capstone

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Must have completed 18 credit hours in the program.

This course is designed to support CUIN mathematics education graduate students as they progress through a systematic investigation of an issue or problem within mathematics education.
Additional Fee: Yes
