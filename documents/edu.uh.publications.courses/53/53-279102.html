SOCW 8325 - Applied Multivariate Statistics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCW 8397 : Biostatistics and doctoral standing or permission from the instructor.

Emphasizes the use of the Statistics Package for Social Sciences (SPSS) in applied social work research.
