MUED 6312 - History and Philosophy of Music Education

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Students in this graduate level MUED course will study both the historical development and current trends of music education in the United States, examine past and present philosophical approaches to music education, and further develop a personal philosophy of music education.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
