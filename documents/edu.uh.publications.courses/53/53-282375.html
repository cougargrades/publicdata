PHLA 6206 - Lean Six Sigma

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

This course will follow the Certified Six Sigma Yellow Belt body of knowledge (BoK) published by the American Society of Quality and will explore the fundamentals of Six Sigma-including information on process improvement project roles and responsibilities, team basics, quality tools, and Six Sigma metrics-before moving into a series of detailed, Yellow Belt-focused topics aligned with the five phases of the Six Sigma DMAIC process (Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, and Control).
Repeatability: No

Note: This class requires a significant level of individual and team performance and demands a high level of professionalism. As part of this class, you are required to participate and take an active role throughout the semester.
Additional Fee: N
