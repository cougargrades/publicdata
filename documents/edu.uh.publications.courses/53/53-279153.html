COMM 6362 - Media and Popular Culture

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

An introduction to the impact of media on popular culture and related theories. This course critically engages with popular culture to understand how an arena often dismissed as mere entertainment can act as an indicator of cultural values, a producer of cultural meaning and influencer of individual and collective identity.
Repeatability: May be repeated multiple times.

Additional Fee: Y
