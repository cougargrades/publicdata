ARTS 6361 - Wood and Metal Fabrication

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course introduces students to a variety of basic methods for making things in wood and metal. From the planning stage to the completion of a project, this class will equip students with the basic skills needed to effectively make their own artworks and objects in wood and metal.
