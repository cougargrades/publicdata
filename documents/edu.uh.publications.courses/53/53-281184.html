COSC 7370 - Network Intrusion Detection

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 6320 or consent of instructor.

Inroduction to intrusion detection, anomaly detection, signature-based detection, automated response to attacks, tracing intruders, user authentication, network tools for intrusion detection, and related computer security issues.
