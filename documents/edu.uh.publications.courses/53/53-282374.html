PHLA 6120 - Leadership Concepts I

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

This longitudinal course will target emerging pharmacy leaders to teach the students principles around human behavior and the impact within the workplace. This class requires a significant level of individual and team performance, and demands a high level of professionalism. As part of this class, you are required to participate and take an active role throughout the semester.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
