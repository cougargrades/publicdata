THEA 6382 - The Teaching of Dramaturgy

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 1.5
Prerequisite: Admission to the Summer MA program.

Survey of the fundamental aspects of dramatic structure and theatrical genres exploring diagnostic tools for better understanding how a given play accomplishes its theatrical goals. Script analysis, context, and pedagogy interwoven.
Additional Fee: Y
