CUIN 7360 - Curriculum Theory

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CUIN 6360 or equivalent or consent of instructor.

Problems in curriculum theory, including the role of the teacher in the curriculum, problems of curriculum design and interrelationships between current issues in curriculum and societal forces.
