IART 7370 - Interdisciplinary Fieldwork

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: None.

Corequisite: IART 6370.

This is the laboratory component of the Interdisciplinary Masterclass. Requires approval of Director of Interdisciplinary Initiatives. This component takes place off campus and is intended for intensive short-term research in the field.
Repeatability: No

Note: Fieldwork associated with IART 6370. This course focuses on site-based research, experiential learning; may involve on and off-campus work.
Additional Fee: No
