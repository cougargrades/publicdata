LAW 6347 - Secured Financing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
This course will focus on the law of secured financing-Article 9 of the Uniform Commercial Code. The course will center around problem sets rather than cases, as problem-solving helps the student to learn and to understand how Article 9 operates in practical situations.
