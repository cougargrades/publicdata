COSC 6351 - Software Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: COSC 6350
Prerequisite: COSC 4351 or consent of the instructor.

In-depth treatment of software engineering topics, including project planning, software processes, metrics, quality assurance techniques, formal methods, and other subjects of current interest.
