THEA 6347 - Scenic Painting

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.0 Lab Contact Hours: 2.0
Prerequisite: None.

Demonstration and practice of theatrical stage painting techniques. Instruction in execution techniques, color theory, and shop best practices as well as preparation for professional opportunities.
Additional Fee: Y
