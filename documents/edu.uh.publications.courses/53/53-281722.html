GEOL 6117 - Structural Geology Laboratory

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: Graduate standing with consent of faculty advisor and credit for or concurrent enrollment in GEOL 6318 .

Corequisite: GEOL 6318 .

Five-day field trip required; expense to be defrayed by student. Techniques for solving problems in structural geology.
Note: This course does not count toward a graduate degree.
