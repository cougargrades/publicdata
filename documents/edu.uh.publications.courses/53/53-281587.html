ELCS 8610 - Laboratory of Practice - Literature & Research Methods Development

Credit Hours: 6.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 6 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Permission of thesis advisor/instructor.

This course develops applied knowledge and application of educational research terminology, perspectives, methodological frameworks, and analytical techniques that support the development of unique thesis chapters containing a) critical literature reviews; and, b)research methods.
