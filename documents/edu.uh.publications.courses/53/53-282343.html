ENGL 7346 - Grammar and Usage

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

This course examines English grammatical features: parts of speech, sentence elements, basic syntax, doctrine of correctness, and usage. Together, we will discuss and explore English grammar, grammatical variations, applications, and implications for language use in daily communication, writing, pedagogy, and English language teaching and learning.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
