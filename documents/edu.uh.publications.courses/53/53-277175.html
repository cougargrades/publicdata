GHL 6344 - California Wine Experience

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Familiarize the student with viticulture and viniculture techniques, as well as production, marketing strategies, business strategies, and corporate culture of California wineries. Visits to numerous wineries including tours, seminars, and tastings.
Additional Fee: Yes
