BTEC 6100 - Seminar in Biotechnology

Credit Hours: 1.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of instructor.

Students are introduced to library, computing and research facilities. Faculty overview the responsible conduct of research, ethics, and describe research methods, and fields in Biotechnology. This course is offered at UH at Sugar Land only.
Repeatability: May be repeated multiple times.
