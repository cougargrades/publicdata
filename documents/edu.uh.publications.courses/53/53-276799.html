PETR 6325 - Integrated Reservoir Characterization

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 5361, 5364, or BS in PETR and PETR Graduate standing or Consent of Program.

Mathematical basis and applications of modern reservoir characterization including pixel-based and object-based geostatistical methods to capture the influence of geology on fluid flow and storage.
