CUIN 7355 - Team and Organizational Leadership

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate status.

This course examines the design, management, and leadership of teams in organizational and educational settings. The focus is on the interpersonal processes and structural characteristics that influence the effectiveness of teams and organizations. The purpose of this course is to understand the theory and processes of leadership so our groups and organizations can learn and continuously improve. This course provides the learner the opportunity to examine their own team and organizational leadership capabilities.
Additional Fee: Yes
