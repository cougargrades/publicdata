SPEC 6371 - Applied Behavior Analysis Assessment & Intervention I

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Previously taken or concurrently enrolled in SPEC 6370.

Discusses Individualized assessment and treatment planning techniques utilized in Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA). Diversity and cultural responsiveness are addressed.
