THEA 6309 - Advanced Theatrical Drafting

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 1.5
Formerly/Same as: THEA 6390 - Technical Drawing.
Prerequisite: None.

This course explores advanced 2D drafting techniques an begins the transition to 3D drafting, utilizing industry standard software and techniques.
