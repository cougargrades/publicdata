COSC 6388 - Network Intrusion Detection

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in computer science or consent of the instructor.

Introduction to intrusion detection, anomaly detection, signature-based detection, automated response to attacks, network tools for intrusion detection, malware detection, graph model of cyber behaviors, and related computer security issues.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
