FINA 7A41 - Fixed Income II

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 7A40.

This course covers models and techniques to price interest rate derivatives and securitized products. This will include basics of interest rate futures and options; modeling techniques such as Vasicek and Black, Derman, Toy and the basics of securitization with a focus on Mortgage Backed Securities (MBS).
