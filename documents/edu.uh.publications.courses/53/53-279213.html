MUSI 6306 - Analysis of 20th- and 21st-Century Music

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Focused analysis of music of the 20th- and 21st-centuries, including a review of analytical trends. Concert music and pop/rock music are discussed from variety of perspectives.
Additional Fee: Y
