CUIN 7368 - Digital Imaging in Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Basic computer literacy.

Application of digital photography, scanning and imaging in a variety of educational contexts, including investigation of current research, theoretical frameworks and instructional practice.
