LAW 5270 - Alternative Energy Transactions and Contracting

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course is designed to familiarize students with some of the documents which are in common use in the domestic, on shore oil and gas industry, including oil and gas leases, operating agreements, farmout agreements and several other forms of agreements.
Additional Fee: Y
