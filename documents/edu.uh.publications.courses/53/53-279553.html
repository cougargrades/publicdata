COSC 6354 - Software Development Practices

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 6311 and COSC 6353 or approval of the instructor.

Pragmatics of software development, hands-on iterative, incremental and agile software development team project with emphasis on use of state of the art tools, techniques, principles, and better practices. Students must make an oral presentation on aspects of the class.
