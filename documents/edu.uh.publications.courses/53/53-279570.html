COSC 6373 - Computer Vision

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 6320 or consent of the instructor.

Introduction to computer vision and machine perception. Topics to be covered include: image formation, representation, image segmentation, feature extraction and analysis, shape representation, binocular stereo and motion analysis.
