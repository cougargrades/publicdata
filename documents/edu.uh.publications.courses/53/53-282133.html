PHAR 5325 - Literature Evaluation/Research Design/Statistics/Epidemiology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Second Year Standing in the Professional Program.

Corequisite: PHAR 5195 Pharmacy Skills Program III.

This course will instill essential skills to help students analyze, interpret and critically evaluate medical literature and answer patient care or drug related questions.
