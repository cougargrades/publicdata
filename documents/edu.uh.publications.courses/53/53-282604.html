CIS 6371 - Design of Data Analytics Solutions

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

The course is designed for students of all backgrounds to gain advanced skills to acquire, manage, analyze, and understand complex data using state-of-the-art data mining and machine learning techniques. The course will use Python or a similar Programming language. Hands-on experiences and practical case studies are used to explore the use of data in decision making.
Additional Fee: N
