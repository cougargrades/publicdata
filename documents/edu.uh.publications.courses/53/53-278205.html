PHAR 5402 - Pharmacology I

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHAR 4421 , and PHAR 4401 .

Study of the mechanism of action of drugs used to modulate the endocrine and autonomic nervous system, and for the treatment of diseases, including allergic disorders, asthma, and heart disease.
