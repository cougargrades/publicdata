BIOL 6312 - Scientific Communication

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor

In this course, we explore different means of relaying scientific information, focusing of the basics of effective data visualization, writing and oral presentations.
