MUED 6313 - Psychology for Musicians

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course investigates how psychology and psychological research intersect with our experiences performing, teaching, listening to, and thinking about music. Areas of focus will include creativity, music perception and cognition, musical development from infancy to adulthood, teaching and learning in music, performance science, positive (e.g., absolute pitch, synesthesia), and negative (e.g., performance anxiety, dystonia) outlier experiences.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
