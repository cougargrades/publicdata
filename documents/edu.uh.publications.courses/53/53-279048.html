SOCW 7330 - Nonprofit Fiscal Management and Budgeting

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of the MSW foundation, or consent of advisor.

This course discusses, analyzes, and implements financing and budgeting theories and techniques applicable to planning, operating, and developing social services.
Additional Fee: Y
