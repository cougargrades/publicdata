NURS 8303 - Complex Systems and Leadership

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: None.

Focuses on the complexity in the healthcare system that requires complex responses from leadership. Using complexity science, interprofessional solutions to complex system issues will be designed within the context of relevant ethical, political, economic, and cultural factors.
Note: Online Course Delivery
