NURS 8356 - Scholarly Project Development/Implementation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: None.

Relates evidence-based strategies for development and implementation of a healthcare improvement plan for populations in a clinical setting. Examples include a significant pilot study, a program evaluation project, a quality improvement project, or a practice change initiative derived from translational science and the DNP role. This project serves as a foundation for future scholarly practice and is reviewed and evaluated by an academic community.
