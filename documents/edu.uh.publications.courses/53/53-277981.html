OPTO 5272 - Optometry II

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: OPTO 5111 , OPTO 5171 , OPTO 5271 , and OPTO 5314

Corequisite: concurrent enrollment in OPTO 5172 .

Epidemiology of ametropias, classification, diagnosis, management, and treatment of ametropias, optical principles of diagnostic instrumentation, objective and subjective examination of refractive errors and binocular vision anomalies, application of psychophysical methods to the clinical examination and near lens determination for presbyopia.
