LAW 6326 - Diplomacy for Oil and Gas

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: N/A.

This course features the crossroads between geopolitics and law. We will study the legal instruments that have been built to govern inter-state and state-corporate relations in the oil and gas industry, providing to the students political and economy background for investment strategies of the oil industry.
Note: Course may be repeated with permission from the Law Center Office of Admissions.
