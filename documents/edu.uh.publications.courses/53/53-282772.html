PHOP 7177 - MATLAB programming for Vision Science Research Project

Credit Hours: 1.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 1.0
Prerequisite: PHOP 7276 or course with similar content and goals if approved by the instructor.

Corequisite: Registered in research practicuum in mentor’s lab.

The course teaches a student to apply MATLAB programming concepts and skills in developing a program for his or her own research project to acquire or analyze data, or both.
Additional Fee: N
