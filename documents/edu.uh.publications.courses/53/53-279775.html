MATH 6376 - Num Linear Algebra

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 6371 or consent of instructor.

Advanced techniques for the direct and iterative solution of linear systems, especially sparse systems, and for the solution of Eigen value problems.
