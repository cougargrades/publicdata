CIS 6358 - Secure Software Design

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ITEC 5321.

Examines the management of software engineering processes with an emphasis on security, including common bugs and tools to prevent them in a secure development process.
Additional Fee: Y
