EDBA 8305 - Managing Change, Technology and Innovation

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing. Only students enrolled in the DBA program are permitted to register to this course.

In this course, students will focus on theories and models of change management and strategy-culture alignment. The class also will rely on design thinking tools to examine how executives can utilize technology innovation and/or entrepreneurship to design innovative organizations, novel products, cutting-edge processes, advanced business models, and sound industry ecosystems.
Additional Fee: N
