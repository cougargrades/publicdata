MIS 7352 - Driving Value with Healthcare Information Technology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

An examination of healthcare organizations from the perspective of managing and using information systems within the enterprise, including statistical and operations research techniques to make evidence-based healthcare business decisions and enhance efficiency.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
