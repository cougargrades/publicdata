ECE 6112 - Department Colloquium

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This is a one credit hour version the Department Colloquium course.
Repeatability: YES

Note: Our department wishes to introduce the two courses ECE 6012 and 6112 under the name “Department Colloquium”, to meet the needs of our students. These two courses will be the same course, and have the same title, allowing our students to choose between enrolling in a zero credit hour version or a one-credit hour version.
Additional Fee: N
