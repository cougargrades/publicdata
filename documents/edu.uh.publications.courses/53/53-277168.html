GHL 6330 - Statistical Data Analysis in the Hospitality Industry

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Basic principles of statistics necessary to conduct hospitality research. Primary topics addressed include links between theory, research design and statistical techniques; variable measurement and frequency distributions; summary statistics for central tendency and variation; hypothesis testing, and bivariate correlation and regression analysis.
Additional Fee: Yes
