CORR 6340 - Cathodic Protection

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CORR 6360 Materials and Corrosion OR equivalent.

Corequisite: None.

This course provides knowledge and techniques for testing and evaluating data to determine the effectiveness of both galvanic and impressed current Cathodic Protection (CP) systems and to gather design data. Also provides a strong focus on interpretation of CP Data, trouble shooting and migration of problems that arise in both galvanic and impressed current systems, including design calculations for these systems. Understanding the principles and procedures for CP design on a variety of structures for both galvanic and impressed current systems.
Repeatability: Y
