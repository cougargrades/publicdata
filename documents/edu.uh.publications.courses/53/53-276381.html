CUIN 7335 - Teaching Mathematics to Multilingual Learners

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course is about the assets that multilingual students bring to the mathematics classroom and research-based recommendations for supporting multilinguals students who are learning English and mathematics. Students in this course will explore community knowledge, culturally relevant tasks, and existing curricular materials in order to improve access for multilingual students.
Additional Fee: Yes
