LAW 5601 - Immigration Clinic I

Credit Hours: 6.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 6.0
Prerequisite: None.

As a student in this clinic, you will represent an asylum seeker from the first client interview, all the way through to the hearing in front of an immigration judge. You will be your client¿s attorney and therefore responsible for all aspects of the case¿client interviewing and counseling, fact investigation and development, working with expert witnesses, legal research and drafting documents, negotiations, and trial advocacy.
Repeatability: May be repeated multiple times.

Additional Fee: N
