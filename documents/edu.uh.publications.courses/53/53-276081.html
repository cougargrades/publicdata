FINA 7327 - Entrepreneurial Finance

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 6335 or FINA 6A35.

The course explores the impact of Finance on early stage companies over time and examines current trends, including within different industries.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
