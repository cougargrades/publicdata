CUIN 7393 - Practicum for the Reading Specialist

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Must be accepted as a candidate for the reading specialist certification.

The primary focus and purpose of the practicum is assuring breadth and quality of training designed to help the reading specialist candidate develop those competencies necessary for professional functioning as a reading specialist in schools. Course Notes: Students in the program must enroll in one semester of practicum and successfully complete 160 hours of supervised practicum experience in a public school to complete program requirements and to partially meet state requirements for certification as a Reading Specialist.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
