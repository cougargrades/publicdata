FINA 7A34 - Mergers & Acquisitions II

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 7A33.

This is the second of two half-semester courses part of a semester-long sequence on mergers and acquisitions (M&As). The course emphasizes financial engineering in M&A deals, and the valuation of price-protection mechanisms (e.g., floors, caps and collars) using options valuation techniques. The course also covers exit and restructuring strategies like divestitures and spin-offs, and introduces students to real options valuation.
