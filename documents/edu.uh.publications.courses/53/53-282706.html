ARTS 6362 - Moldmaking

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: None.

The process of moldmaking and casting allows artists to easily make copies of objects. This course teaches how to make molds and objects in materials like plaster, concrete, resin, wax, silicone and even metal. Students come away with the ability and confidence to undertake casting projects from planning to completion of the project.
Additional Fee: N
