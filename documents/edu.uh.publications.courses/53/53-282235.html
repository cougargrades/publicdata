ETIM 6310 - Fundamentals of Innovation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Fundamental skills to create, communicate, and implement innovations in any field, including how to generate unique solutions to problems, define and communicate those solutions to stakeholders, test the viability of innovations, and use principles of system design and thinking to lead systems for innovation.
