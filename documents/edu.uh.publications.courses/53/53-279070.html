SOCW 7366 - Grief/Bereave Therapy

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of the MSW foundation, or consent of advisor.

This course examines stages of grief and bereavement with a variety of social treatment interventions to assist the bereaved client in dealing with the grieving process.
Additional Fee: Y
