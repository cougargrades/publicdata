MUED 8330 - Quantitative Research Methods

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of MUED 6301.

Addresses quantitative research methods through an in-depth exploration and application of design, methodology, analysis, and interpretation within music education. Topics include standard empirical research design, research ethics, data collection, analytic methods, quality evaluation, and best practices.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
