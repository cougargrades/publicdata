ELET 6325 - Practicum in Engineering Technology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Completion of 12 graduate credits, a 3.25 GPA and consent of advisor. Work experience in a pre-approved industrial site/research faciltity.

Analyze, integrate, improve, organize and manage a complex system relevant to the program.
Note: Course may be repeated twice.
Additional Fee: Y
