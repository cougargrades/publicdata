GEOL 6322 - Geophysical Programming

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2414 and PHYS 2326 or equivalent.

Hands-on introduction to procedural programming for geoscientific applications: data manipulation, basics of numerical modeling, workflow management, data plotting.
