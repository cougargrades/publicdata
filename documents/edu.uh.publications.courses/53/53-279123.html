COMM 6308 - Persuasion

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Theories and research on the persuasive effects of media messages and technological affordances, as well as their implications for strategic and health communication practices.
Repeatability: May be repeated multiple times.

Additional Fee: Y
