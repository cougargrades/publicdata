PSYC 7309 - Applied Longitudinal Analysis in Social and Health Psychology

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: PSYC 6300 and PSYC 6302 or consent of instructor.

Students will learn how to analyze social and health psychology data involving three or more time points. Topics will cover multilevel growth curve, latent growth curve, and daily diary analyses.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
