INDE 7390 - Supply Chain Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: graduate standing and/or consent of instructor.

Topics include: inventory and risk pooling, networking planning, value of information, supply chain integration, distribution strategies, strategic alliances, procurement and outsourcing strategies, global logics and risk management.
