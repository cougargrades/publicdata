ENGL 6302 - Creative Writing Pedagogy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

This course will cover current trends in the teaching of creative writing, as well as some history and theory to contextualize the issues at hand. Topics will include some standard concerns such as conducting workshops, organizing materials, managing assessments, using craft texts, teaching revision, the teacher/writer balancing act, engaging student texts in various stages of creative composition, as well as consideration of experimentation and newer classroom strategies such as online creative writing tasks and courses.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
