CUIN 6359 - Museum Education Practice & Application II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CUIN 6358 and CUIN 7302

Advanced practicum course where students address issues concerning current museum practices. Students and their museum partner identify a capstone project to demonstrate understanding of museum practice, along with the development and implementation of museum learning.
