PHAR 4280 - Medication/Patient Safety and Informatics

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: First year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

This course is designed to provide students with the knowledge of basic concepts of medication safety, patient safety, quality improvement and informatics to optimize the patient care process.
