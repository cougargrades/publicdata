SOCW 7379 - Abolitionist Perspectives in Social Work

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Develops knowledge, values, and skills to integrate abolitionist theory and praxis into the social work profession as a means toward achieving justice and liberation.
