LAW 5207 - Public Health Law

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course provides an in-depth examination of public health law principles and challenges. Students will explore constitutional issues related to public health and develop an understanding of the state¿s public health powers and duties as they are balanced against an individual’s legally protected rights.
