BTEC 6105 - Bioprocessing Laboratory

Credit Hours: 1.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of instructor.

Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in BTEC 6305.

Laboratory experiments will cover upstream and downstream techniques of product processing including cell culture and protein purification. Students will learn to follow Standard Operating Procedures in executing the production of a test protein in a bioreactor, chromatographic purification, and the testing of product quality attributes.
