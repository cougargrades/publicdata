LAW 6341 - Water Law

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Explores the way rights to use are allocated in the various jurisdictions of the United States, and examines acquisition and exercise of water rights, statutory procedures of acquisition, comparison of riparian rights and appropriation rights.
