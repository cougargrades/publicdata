LAW 5424 - Death Penalty Clinic

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: completion LAW 5303 & LAW 5488 .

Explores the substantive law, investigative techniques, & post-conviction appellate remedies applicable in capital (death penalty cases).
