PHAR 5403 - Pharmacology II

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHAR 5402 .

The mechanism of action of chemotherapeutic drugs used to treat infection and cancer and for the treatment of diseases, including anxiety, convulsions, psychosis, depression, ADHD, obesity, and degenerative diseases.
