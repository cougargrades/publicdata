FINA 7A61 - Personal Financial Planning

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 6A35.

Provides a broad understanding of personal financial planning with an emphasis on practical applications. Focuses on integral personal financial planning topics with an emphasis on investments.
