FINA 7349 - Capstone in Personal Financial Planning

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and permission of the program director.

The course is designed specifically for students pursuing a career in the financial planning and wealth management profession and seeking the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER designation. Students develop comprehensive financial plans in this case-based course by exploring topics studied in previous courses in the Financial Planning and Wealth Management specialization. Students will analyze a client¿s personal financial situation in detail and make appropriate observations and recommendations so the client would obtain their stated financial objectives.
Additional Fee: N
