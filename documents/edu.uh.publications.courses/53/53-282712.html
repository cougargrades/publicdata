COMD 8197 - Selected Topics in COMD

Credit Hours: 1.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Admission to the PhD program in Communication Sciences and Disorders or instructor approval.

This course will explore the clinical and experimental literature as well as theoretical issues on various topics within the field of communication sciences and disorders.
Repeatability: May be repeated multiple times.

Additional Fee: N
