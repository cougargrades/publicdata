ARED 7350 - Critical Visual Culture

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course explores key concepts, themes, and critical approaches in visual culture to understand images and the contexts in which they operate from a cross-disciplinary perspective. Emphasis is placed on in-depth investigation of contemporary visual culture practices for developing critical agency, effective visual communication, and informed practice.
Additional Fee: Y
