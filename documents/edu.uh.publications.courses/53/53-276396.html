CUIN 7356 - Issues in Distance Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None

Examines the concepts, technologies and issues related to the development and delivery of distance education. Topics include theory and history, distance education programs throughout the world, components of a distance education system, course design and development, technology and media, instruction and interaction, the student and teacher, and administration and policy-making in distance education.
