MUSI 6250 - Performance Pedagogy

Credit Hours: 2.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in music.

Students will develop a comprehensive understanding of teaching tools, materials, and resources designed for beginning and intermediate students. Key topics include an overview of current teaching resources and methods, effective teaching methodologies, communication skills, curriculum planning, informal music learning, leveling repertoire, and participation in peer-teaching and observational activities.
Additional Fee: N
