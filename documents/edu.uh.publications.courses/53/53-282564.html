PHYS 6301 - Fundamental Electrodynamics

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Consent of the Chair of the Physics Graduate studies Committee.

This is an introductory graduate course covering the fundamental electromagnetic theory required to take the Core course, Electrodynamics (PHYS 6321), for MS and PhD students.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be applied toward a graduate degree. The students are required to do a graduate level course work or project as a part of this course.
Additional Fee: No
