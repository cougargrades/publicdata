LAW 5251 - VOIR DIRE: The Art of Juror De-Selection

Credit Hours: 2.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

The most important part of any trial is picking your jury - the audience that will hear your presentation and ultimately decide your case. In this class, the students will learn about the psychology as well as the law behind choosing a jury for any kind of case. Through practical exercises as well as a study of the applicable legal principles and case law.
Additional Fee: N
