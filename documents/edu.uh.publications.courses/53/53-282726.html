EDBA 8309 - Generating Business Insights using Qualitative Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing. Only students enrolled in the DBA program are permitted to register to this course.

In this course, students will learn the basic principles of qualitative techniques, such as case studies, action research, grounded theory building and content analysis. They also will cover interviewing and observation techniques, coding semi-structured and structured interview data.
