NURS 8208 - DNP Seminar I

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2
Postulates knowledge and skills to facilitate integration and synthesis of the essential specialty competencies necessary to plan and design a DNP Project. Provides opportunity for synthesis of evidence for a theory based organizational intervention at multiple system levels. The activities will be specific to the student’s identified area of specialty. Students will share issues related to the plan and design of the DNP Project in seminar.
Note: Online Course Delivery
