PCEU 6245 - Advanced Pharmaceutics II

Credit Hours: 2.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: PCEU 3340 and PCEU 3341 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.

A systematic study of the current development of innovative delivery systems. Topics include solid dosage forms, dispersed systems, dermatopharmaceutics, controlled release dosage forms, liposomes, drug targeting delivery and biotechnology-derived products.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
