LAW 5396 - Elections and the Law of Democracy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: N/A.

This course covers the law that structures democratic politics and the processes of democracy, with a primary focus on constitutional law and election law.
Note: Course may be repeated with permission from the Law Center Office of Admissions.
