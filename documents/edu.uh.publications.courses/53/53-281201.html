LAW 6223 - Drafting & Negotiating Int’l Oil & Gas Agreements

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

International oil and gas agreements will be discussed while providing practical experience in drafting and negotiation. Students will learn how an international oil and gas lawyer approaches oil/gas industry challenges.
