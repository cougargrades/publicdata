CIVE 7377 - Environmental Nanochemistry

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: CIVE 3331 and CIVE 6377 or permission of instructor.

The synthesis and characterization of nanomaterials, their aqueous stability, fate and transport in the environment, and the implications and applications of nanomaterials.
