PHLS 6323 - Psychopathology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course will explore criteria of psychiatric diagnoses, use of the current Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders and theories of psychopathology. The course will also include basic knowledge of types of psychopharmacological medications.
