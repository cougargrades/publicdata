PHAR 4320 - Physiology I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: First year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

Corequisite: PHAR 4250 (formerly PHAR 4150 ).

Principles of human anatomy and physiology of the nervous systems including fetal physiology; an introduction to neuroanatomy, neurophysiology.
