OPTO 6312 - Optics IV

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: OPTO 6311 .

Optics of contact lenses, vertical imbalance and its correction, absorptive lenses, prism, anisometropia, and aniseikonia.
