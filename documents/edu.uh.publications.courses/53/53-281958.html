BZAN 6351 - Basic Programming for Business Analytics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and admission to the MS/BZAN program.

Completion of or concurrent enrollment in BZAN 6350 or approval of chair. Course covers programming techniques for data management. Languages include popular open-source programming languages, e.g., R programming.
Additional Fee: No
