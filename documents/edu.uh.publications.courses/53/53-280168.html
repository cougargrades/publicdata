LAW 7309 - WRS: Climate Intervention Law and Policy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Climate change poses one of the greatest threats of our time, but our legal and regulatory systems have enormous difficulties in responding to it. As a result, the consequences of climate change are inexorably growing while global emissions of greenhouse gases from human activity continue to accelerate.
Additional Fee: Yes
