CIVE 6384 - Principles of satellite altimetry, gravimetry and InSAR with their applications in earth sciences

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.

Principles of range estimation from altimetry; altimetry waveform retracking; elevation changes of inland water, ice sheets, and sea surface from satellite altimetry; Earth’s gravitational field and reference fields; principles of satellite gravimetry; terrestrial water storage changes from satellite gravimetry; principles of interferometric synthetic aperture radar and its processing; applications of InSAR for hydrology.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
