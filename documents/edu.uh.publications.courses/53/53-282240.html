FINA 7A37 - Corporate Strategy-Equity Fund Management

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing, FINA 6A35 or FINA 6335, and instructor permission.

This course analyzes the effects of industry competition and corporate strategy on equity valuation. Using analytic approaches and case studies, the course covers the relation of industry structure and product market strategy to sustainable competitive advantage and equity return performance.
