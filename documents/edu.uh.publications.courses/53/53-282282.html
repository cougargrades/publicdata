MUSI 6371 - Analysis of Art Song

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 6341 or consent of instructor.

This course examines the nineteenth-century German art song of the Lied tradition. Composers covered include Franz Schubert, Robert Schumann, Clara Schumann, Johannes Brahms, Fanny Hensel, and Josephine Lang. By the end of the semester, students should be familiar with analysis of German Romantic poetry, chromatic harmony, text setting, text expression, form, and performance analysis.
