PHAR 5236 - Integrated Immunology Module

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing in the professional curriculum.

Corequisite: PHAR 5268.

This course will help integrate the pathophysiology, medicinal chemistry, pharmacology, toxicology, and therapeutics in the clinical management of common Immunology diseases/conditions.
