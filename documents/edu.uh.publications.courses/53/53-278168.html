PHAR 5208 - Infect Disease Pharmacotherapy

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing in the College of Pharmacy or consent of instructor.

Integration of clinical pharmacological, pharmacokinetic, toxicological and therapeutic properties, antimicro agents and pharmacoeconomic principles as they relate to the selection and clinical use of drugs in the treatment of infectious diseases.
