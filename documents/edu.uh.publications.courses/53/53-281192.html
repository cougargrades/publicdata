MATH 7381 - Applied and Computational Stochastic Processes

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Probability.

Discrete-time and continuous-time Markov chains, Poisson process, diffusions and analysis of mutliscale systems. This course places emphasis on applications and computational assignments.
Repeatability: No
