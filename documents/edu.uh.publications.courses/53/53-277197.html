GHL 6381 - Strategic Decisions Making in the Hospitality Industry

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

An introduction to hospitality business research methods and quantitative analysis for decision making. Topics include defining research questions to address management issues, research study design, sampling plans and data collection.
Additional Fee: No
