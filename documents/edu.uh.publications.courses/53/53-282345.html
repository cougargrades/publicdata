ENGL 8392 - Topics in Poetics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

This course will explore a variety of topics centering on poetry and the study of poetics. Depending on the instructor, the theme, theory, and period of exploration will change, but the central focus on poetry will remain.
Repeatability: YES

Additional Fee: N
