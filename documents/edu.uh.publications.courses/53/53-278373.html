MECT 6340 - Materials Selection and Management

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: MECT 4372 or equivalent, or consent of the instructor.

Material selection as part of the design process. Includes analysis of material properties, process and process selection, hybrid materials, and case studies.
Additional Fee: Y
