CORR 6360 - Materials and Corrosion

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3345 Materials Science or equivalent course.

Corequisite: None.

This course provides an overview of materials and corrosion. The basics of corrosion with emphasis on the type of corrosion and materials degradation that can occur in oil and gas production are included. Materials addressed include metallic, non-metallic, and coatings. Erosion, a problem associated with high rate production encountered in oil and gas production will be addressed. The critical use of industry codes, standards, recommended practice and project specifications will be discussed and recommendations given.
Repeatability: Y
