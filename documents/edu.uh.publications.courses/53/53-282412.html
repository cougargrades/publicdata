ARLD 6373 - Management, Administration and Leadership in Public Art

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

The course will serve as an overview to the administration, management and leadership of Public Art as a professional field for leaders in arts organizations and for individual artists. Through site visits and guest lectures from leaders in the sector, the ourse will encourage students to determine the importance of planning and decision making and will deepen understanding of policies and political realities that affect the creation and implemtation of art in puclic spaces. Course Notes: This courses can be taken as an elective for the Arts Leadership degree program or as a course for the Museum and Gallery Management Certificate.
Additional Fee: No
