PHOP 8X65 - Advanced Topical Seminar in Physiological Optics and Visual Sciences

Credit Hours: 2.0 - 3.0
Reading scientific papers in a topical area of physiological optics and visual sciences; preparing written reviews and making oral presentations; critiquing papers, reviews, and presentations.
