LAW 5118 - Environment, Energy & Natural Resources Research

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This is a practical class, focused on finding the law and legal authorities relevant to taking and defending actions concerning the environment, energy, and natural resources.
