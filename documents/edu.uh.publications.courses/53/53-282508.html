ECON 7353 - Human Capital

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ECON 7342 and ECON 7331 or consent of instructor.

Review of structural models of human capital formation from static binary choice models of school choices to life-cycle models with human capital accumulation. Provides training in formulating, solving, and estimating dynamic heterogeneous-agents models with discrete and continuous choices. Intended for second- and third-year Ph.D. students in the Economics department.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
