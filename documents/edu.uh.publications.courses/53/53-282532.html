HIST 6302 - The Global History of Gender

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Permission of the instructor.

This course takes a global approach to the study of women’s and gender history. Students read theoretical and historiographical approaches to women’s and gender history, considering new and foundational scholarship, in order to identify gendered structures and connections across time and space.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
