NURS 8215 - Advanced Leadership and Management Seminar

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: None.

Corequisite: NURS 8316.

Focuses on knowing oneself as a leader, forecasting future needs, understanding the healthcare team, coaching others, balancing data -driven decisions with innovation, and a thorough understanding of the complexity of the evolving healthcare environment.
Note: Online Course Delivery
