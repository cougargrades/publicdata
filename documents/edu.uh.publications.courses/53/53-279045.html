SOCW 7326 - Disparities in Health in America: Working Toward Social Justice

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of the MSW foundation, or consent of advisor.

This course presents a comprehensive bio-psychosocial approach for understanding and addressing health disparities in America.
