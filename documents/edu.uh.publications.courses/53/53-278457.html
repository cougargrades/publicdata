PEP 8314 - Research Methods and Design in KIN

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

This course covers theories and concepts related to research methods in the context of sport, health, and exercise science (SHES). Specific focus will be given to action components of the research process including: design and formulation, research strategies, and methodological tools necessary to perform research. Also, fundamentals in the understanding of data analyses and results interpretations will be handled throughout the semester.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
