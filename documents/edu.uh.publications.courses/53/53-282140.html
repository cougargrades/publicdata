PETR 6352 - Shale Reservoirs

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR graduate standing and PETR 6351 , 6328, 6362, 6364 or BS in Petroleum Engineering.

Understand the fundamental differences among the various types of shale reservoirs: Apply basic open hole logs to shale reservoir evaluation, integrate wireline logging data with basic core data in order to assess fundamental, properties influencing shale reservoir productivity, particularly TOC and brittleness.
