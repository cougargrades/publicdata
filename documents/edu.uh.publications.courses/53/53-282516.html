ELCS 6391 - Practicum in the Principalship

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

The primary purpose of this course is to prepare principals who understand and demonstrate the ability to identify, reflect upon, and articulate ethical beliefs and values; assume the roles and functions of school-based management; apply appropriately various leadership theories; articulate personal understanding of the quality of schooling they wish to achieve for the community of the school they are to lead; apply human relations skills in interacting effectively with others; and analyze and solve problems using appropriate decision-making techniques.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
