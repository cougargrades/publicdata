LAW 6346 - Payment Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
This course explores and compares the law of various “money substitutes”, with emphasis on the check system. The allocations of risk by the check system, the credit card system, the debit card system, the wire transfer system, and the letter of credit system are compared and contrasted.
