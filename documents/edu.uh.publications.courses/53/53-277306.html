LAW 5305 - Business Torts

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
This course will cover torts that typically cause pure economic harm (i.e., harms to the pocketbook, rather than harms to the person or property. The course will emphasize the operation of various business torts and will examine the torts’ applicability to particular business or economic settings.
