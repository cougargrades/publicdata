MANA 7395 - Practical Experiences in Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and admission to the Online MS in Management and Leadership.

This course uses practical experiences to apply the fundamental principles of management and leadership. These practical experiences may include internships, projects, and experiential courses such as study abroad courses.
