PHLS 7301 - Practicum

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PHLS 7100
Prerequisite: None.

This course is designed to prepare the student in the practical application and integration of the principles and methods of counseling. Students will work with clients under the supervision of both the faculty instructor and an approved, licensed site supervisor to develop basic counseling skills and integrate professional knowledge.
