GEOL 6312 - The Mechanics of Sediment Transport

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

Physical interactions between fluid flow and sediment. Covers grain-, bedform-, and landscape-scale dynamics. Examination of sediment transport for rivers, deltas, coastlines, estuaries, and deserts. Evaluates diagnostic sedimentary features and implications for preservation.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
