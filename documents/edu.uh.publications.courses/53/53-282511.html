ECON 8372 - Topics in Education

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ECON 7342 and ECON 7331 or consent of instructor.

Topics include human capital, signaling, estimation of returns to schooling. Examination of education production functions and efficacy of school spending, teachers, and peers on educational outcome as well as parental investments and intergenerational mobility. Policy questions: what are the most effective human capital policies that will meet the ever-increasing demand for an educated workforce? Which policies reduce inequality and enhance economic mobility?
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
