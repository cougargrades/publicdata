PHLS 6312 - Crisis Counseling

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course will educate and train students about the psychosocial aspects of individual and group adjustment to crisis situations/traumatic experiences. Students will learn to provide effective mental health interventions for trauma survivors and their communities.
