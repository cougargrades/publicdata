GHL 6367 - Hotel Sales

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course focuses on the functions of a hotel sales department including group, transient and banquet sales, selected sales and customer service techniques, selling styles, and facility presentations.
Additional Fee: No
