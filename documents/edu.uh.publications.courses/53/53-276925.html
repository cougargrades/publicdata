ECE 6346 - Vlsi Design

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECE 3456 or ECE 3457, or graduate standing and/or consent of instructor.

Integrated circuit design using computer aided design methods; MOS, GaAs and bipolar techniques, standard cells, digital subcircuit and memory layout and design.
