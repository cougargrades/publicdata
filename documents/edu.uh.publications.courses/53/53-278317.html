BTEC 6302 - Introduction to Regulatory Affairs

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of instructor.

Overview of federal and global regulations of biotechnology products. Topics include: history, regulatory agencies, drugs, biologics, and medical device submissions, GLP, GCP, GMP, and FDA inspections.
Note: This course is offered at UH at Sugar Land only.
Additional Fee: Y Fee Type Y
