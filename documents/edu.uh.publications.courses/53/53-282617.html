PHAR 4250 - Pharmacy Skills Program I

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: First year standing in the curriculum.

Corequisite: PHAR 4270, PHAR 4330, PHAR 4320, PHAR 4260.

To practice communicating to patients, caregivers and other healthcare providers in a simulated pharmacy setting as well as being able to utilize drug information resources and databases to answer drug and healthcare related problems and drug information questions and to apply mathematical skills required to accurately prepare compounds and prescriptions, including individualized drug dosing, that are therapeutically safe for patient use.
Repeatability: Y
