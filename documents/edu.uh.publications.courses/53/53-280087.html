LAW 5228 - Judicial Externship I

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: None

The Judicial Externship Program enables students to experience and reflect upon the law in practice through field placements within state and federal judges chambers. The programs goal is to expose students to the disposition of real world legal issues, while working under the supervision of a judge and experienced attorneys.
