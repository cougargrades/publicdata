SPEC 6362 - Behavior: Evidence-Based Decisions

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in SPEC 6360.

Individualized behavior-analytic strategies such as behavioral observation, assessment, data collection tools, and evidence-based decisions that teachers can implement with learners who emit challenging behaviors across a variety of school, home, and professional settings are presented and practiced. The goal of this course is to use Applied Behavior Analysis to design ethical behavioral interventions that incorporate evidence-based practices and monitor progress of these interventions within a multi-tiered system of support framework.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
