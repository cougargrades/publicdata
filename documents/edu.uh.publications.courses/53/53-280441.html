CUIN 8318 - Issues in Urban Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission to Ph.D. or Ed.D.

This course is an opportunity to engage in critical investigation of issues in urban education and is focused on readings and research in the field.
