PETR 6368 - Well Drilling and Completion I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6351 , 6364 or BS in Petroleum Engineering and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Drilling rig design and operation, drilling programs, drill string and bit design, drilling mud composition, properties and functions, casing design and cementing, methods of well completion.
