LAW 5304 - Complex Litigation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None

This course covers the major procedural issues that arise in complex civil litigation. It will focus primarily on multi-party, multi jurisdictional disputes, with particular emphasis on topics such as class actions, multidistrict litigation (MDL) practice, and other methods of aggregating claims.
