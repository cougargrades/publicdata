BCHS 6239 - Stem Cell Molecular Biology and Applications

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

An overview of Stem Cell Biology including introduction to embryonic development, molecular biology, genetics, and biochemistry for embryonic stem cells, adult stem cells, and cell fate reprogramming.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
