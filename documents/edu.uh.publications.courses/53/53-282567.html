PHYS 6309 - Fundamental Mechanics

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Consent of the Chair of the Physics Graduate Studies Committee.

This is an introductory graduate course covering the fundamentals of classical mechanics required to take the Core course, Advanced Mechanics I (PHYS 6319), for MS and PhD students.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be applied toward a graduate degree. The students are required to do a graduate level course work or project as a part of this course.
Additional Fee: No
