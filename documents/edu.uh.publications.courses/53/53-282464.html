NURS 8304 - Systems Management Vulnerable Populations

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: None.

Focuses on health and well-being as a population phenomenon through the study of the role of social determinants of health and interprofessional systems science to support quality health status and safety of vulnerable populations. The determinants of health and innovative models for impacting health systems will be analyzed and synthesized.
Note: Online Course Delivery
