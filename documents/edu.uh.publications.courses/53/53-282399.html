NURS 8302 - Advanced Practice Finance and Economics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3
Evaluates impact of organizational structure, financial and marketing decisions and their impact on the cost, quality and accessibility of healthcare. Develops advanced skills in analyzing financial statements, managing financial decisions, developing budgets and business plans.
