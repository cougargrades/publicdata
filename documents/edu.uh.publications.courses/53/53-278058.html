PHOP 6160 - General Seminar Visual Sciences

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Reading recent scientific papers in the broad areas of physiological optics and visual sciences; preparing written reviews and making oral presentations; critiquing of papers, reviews, and presentations made by others.
Repeatability: May be repeated for credit.
