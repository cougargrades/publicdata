PETR 6326 - Applied Reservoir Simulation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6328 , 6351, 6362, 6364 or BS in Petroleum Engineering, PETR 6302 and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

A comprehensive study of all aspects of completing a numerical reservoir study. Students will use commercial software to conduct and present their own history-match using a real-world case study.
