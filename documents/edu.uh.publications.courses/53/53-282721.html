EDBA 8304 - Research Symposium

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing. Only students enrolled in the DBA program are permitted to register to this course.

In this course, students will be exposed to a set of inter-disciplinary topics based on the cutting-edge research conducted by Bauer faculty and doctoral students. Each student will present individual research and obtain constructive feedback.
Additional Fee: N
