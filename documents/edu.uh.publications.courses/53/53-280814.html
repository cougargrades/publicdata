MUSA 8320 - Doctoral Applied Music

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: (0-3)
Prerequisite: Doctoral standing in music.

Private lessons in orchestral instruments, piano, organ, harpsichord, voice, or conducting.
Note: One private lesson and one studio class per week.
