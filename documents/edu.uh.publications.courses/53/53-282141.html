PHLS 6311 - Introduction to Counseling

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course aims to provide an accurate picture of the counseling profession in its contemporary environment. Specifically, students will develop their professional identities as clinical mental health counselors by being provided with a history of the profession and the key tenets that shape the perspectives of mental health counselors.
