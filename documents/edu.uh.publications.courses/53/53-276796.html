PETR 6320 - Enhanced Oil Recovery Processes I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6328 , 6351, 6362 or BS in Petroleum Engineering, PETR 6302 and PETR Graduate standing or Consent of Program.

Review of water flood calculation methods, extension to polymer flooding, caustic flooding, and carbonated water flooding. Hydrocarbon miscible flooding and CO flooding, estimation of recovery.
