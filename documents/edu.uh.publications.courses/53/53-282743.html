LAW 5133 - U.S. Legal Skills

Credit Hours: 1.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This course will provide foundational information to prepare foreign lawyers for the study of law as U.S. Law LL.M. Program. The course focuses on legal terminology, concepts of professionalism, tools used in preparation for legal study, law school exam preparation in the U.S., the way principles, practices, and theories of law are taught in the U.S, foundations of the U.S. legal system, and procedure and process for interpreting and enforcing laws in the U.S..
Additional Fee: N
