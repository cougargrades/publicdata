COSC 7373 - Advanced Computer Vision

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 6373 or consent of the instructor.

In-depth treatment of computer vision, including physics-based modeling, shape from shading, motion tracking, object recognition and other subjects of current interest.
Repeatability: May be repeated for credit when topics vary.
