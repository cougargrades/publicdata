ECON 8355 - Topics in International Economics

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ECON 7344 and ECON 7331; ECON 7355 and ECON 8396 recommended, or consent of instructor.

Review core trade and open economy macro models; then focus on recent important papers in both areas. Students will give presentations on papers and propose research projects.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
