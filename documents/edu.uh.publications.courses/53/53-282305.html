SCM 7335 - Logistics Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and SCM 6A01 or equivalent.

Infrastructure and planning techniques for moving, storing and distributing goods and services on a global scale. Sample topics are customer service management, transportation, vehicle routing, distribution, e-commerce, network design, forecasting, inventory, logistics technologies, change management, and emerging logistics trends.
