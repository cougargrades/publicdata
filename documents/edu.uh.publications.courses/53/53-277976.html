OPTO 5194 - Ophthalmic Optics Lab

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: OPTO 5111 and OPTO 5314 .

Spectacle lens verification (lensometry), hand neutralization, ophthalmic frames, single vision lenses and multifocals, prism and lens positioning in a frame.
