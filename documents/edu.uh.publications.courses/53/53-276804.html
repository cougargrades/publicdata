PETR 6332 - Deterministic Reserves Estimation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6351, PETR 6362 or BS in Petroleum Engineering and PETR graduate standing or consent of program.

Deterministic & Probabilistic methods of reserves estimation, including volumetric, analogy, material balance, decline curves, and reservoir simulation. Statistical methods for estimating proved undeveloped reserves in resource plays. Quantifying uncertainty in reserves estimates.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
