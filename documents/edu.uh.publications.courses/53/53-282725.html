EDBA 8308 - Application of Quantitative Methods

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing. Only students enrolled in the DBA program are permitted to register to this course.

In this course, students will cover different multivariate techniques and tools for data analysis, with a focus on practical business applications and research design. The class is project based; each student will work on a project focused on the student¿s individualized topic of interest.
