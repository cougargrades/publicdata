ELCS 6321 - Instructional Leadership II

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

The focus of Instructional leadership II is instructional coaching and mentoring for school leaders. This course provides aspiring school leaders with the knowledge and skills to lead teacher teams in the improvement of instructional practice and student learning and in understanding the role of leaders as coaches that create targeted instructional improvements with faculty. In addition, this course provides school leaders with the knowledge and skills necessary for clinical coaching and mentoring of instructional faculty.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
