MANA 7360 - Leading Human Resource Management: Strategy & Policy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

Integrative experiential course in the strategic leadership of human resource policies, activities, and functions.
Repeatability: No

Note: Enrollment is restricted to students who have completed at least 24 hours of graduate level coursework in HRM.
