SPEC 6376 - Theory and Philosophy of Behavior Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: SPEC 6372.

Discusses the history and philosophy of behaviorism, theoretical approaches to understanding behavior, and interpretation of behavior in terms of the concepts and principles of behavior analysis. Diversity and cultural responsiveness are addressed.
Additional Fee: N
