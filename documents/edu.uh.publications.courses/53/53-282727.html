EDBA 8310 - Competing on Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing. Only students enrolled in the DBA program are permitted to register to this course.

In this course, students will identify the challenges and opportunities associated with the use of AI-based systems and business analytics for decision-making. The class is project based; each student works on a project focused on the student¿s individualized topic of interest.
