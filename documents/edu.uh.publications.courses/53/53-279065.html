SOCW 7354 - Spirituality and Aging

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of the MSW foundation, or consent of advisor.

Examines the significance of spirituality over the life course with attention to the theoretical connections among spirituality, religion, and vital aging as well as clinical implications for practice with clients.
Additional Fee: Y
