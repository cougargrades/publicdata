PHAR 5266 - Pharmacy Law

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing in the curriculum.

To understand current legal and ethical issues impacting the practice of pharmacy and to gain a comprehensive understanding of the legal requirements to practice pharmacy in Texas.
