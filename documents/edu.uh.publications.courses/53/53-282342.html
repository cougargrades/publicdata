ENGL 7345 - Language Socialization

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English, or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

By definition, language socialization is how people teach, each other to use language in any given context or social situation. In this course we will study how we are constantly learning to communicate and constantly signaling to others how they should communicate with us in accordance with whatever contexts we encounter them in.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
