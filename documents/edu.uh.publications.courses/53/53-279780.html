MATH 6382 - Probability

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3334, MATH 3338 and MATH 4378, or consent of instructor.

A survey of probability theory and probability models. Includes basic probability theory and introduction to stochastic processes.
Repeatability: No
