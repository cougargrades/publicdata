LAW 7333 - WRS: Law and Social Theory

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

This seminar will teach how to analyze law in conversation with a variety of social theories. We will read a selection of authors from the Frankfurt School of social theory, Harvard Legal Studies movement, Feminism, Psychoanalysis, post-structuralism, post-colonialism and race and law and the course will reflect on the applicability of their social visions and insights to legal adjudication, education, and practice.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
