ACCT 7390 - Control & Security of Financial Information

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and ACCT 3367 or ACCT 5367 or ACCT 6331 or equivalent.

This course provides a broad overview of the information security risks companies face and how to manage those risks. The course builds students’ understanding of the management, operational, and technical controls necessary to ensure the integrity of a company’s financial information.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
