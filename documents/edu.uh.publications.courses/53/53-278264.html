PHCA 7307 - Epidemiologic Methods and Research Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of instructor.

Principles of epidemiologic research methods and applications to address current clinical problems in pharmaceutical health outcomes research. Included are fundamentals of epidemiologic study design, quantitative and qualitative methods, computer applications, design of survey questionnaires, and proficiency in evaluating health policy interventions.
