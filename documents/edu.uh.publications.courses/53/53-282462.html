NURS 8225 - Complex Health Deviations Seminar

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: None.

Integrate theoretical and evidence-based knowledge of assessment, diagnosis, management, and evaluation of patients with increasingly complex acute and chronic health problems across the lifespan.
