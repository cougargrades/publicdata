NURS 8209 - DNP Seminar II

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: None.

Students share issues in a seminar format as the DNP project nears completion. The role of the DNP as leader and innovator in complex organizational systems will be discussed as it relates to implementation and evaluation. Implications of the DNP project as it could influence social justice, professional responsibility, disparity that may affect current healthcare delivery.
Note: Online Course Delivery
