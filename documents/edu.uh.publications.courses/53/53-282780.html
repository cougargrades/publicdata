SPEC 6370 - Basic Principles of Behavior Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Discusses basic concepts and principles of operant and respondent conditioning and the underlying theoretical and philosophical concepts of behavior analysis. Diversity and cultural responsiveness are addressed.
