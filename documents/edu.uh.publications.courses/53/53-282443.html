PETR 6354 - Reservoir Engineering Fundamentals

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in petroleum engineering or consent of department.

Rock and fluid properties, crude oil and natural gas PVT behavior, fundamentals of fluid flow through porous media, principle of superposition, introduction to well test analysis, field performance forecast, introduction to waterflooding and enhanced oil recovery.
Additional Fee: No
