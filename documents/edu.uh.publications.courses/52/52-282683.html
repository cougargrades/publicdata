SPEC 3373 - Methods in Applied Behavior Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPEC 3371 .
Description
Discusses measurement, data display and interpretation, single-case designs, and intervention and program interpretation utilized in Applied Behavior Analysis. Diversity, equity, and inclusion are addressed.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
