INTN 3434 -  Simultaneous Interpreting: Intermediate Skills

Credit Hours: 4
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly ASLI 3334
Prerequisite: INTN majors and INTN 3433 .
Description
Enhanced techniques for skill development when interpreting simultaneously between spoken/written English and American Sign Language. Lab included for intensive interlingual practice.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
