CHEM 2325 - Organic Chemistry II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 2323 and CHEM 1312 .
Description
Chemistry of the compounds of carbon with emphasis on energies and mechanism of reactions, synthesis, and the structure of organic molecules.
Repeatability: No

Note: May not apply toward degree until CHEM 2125 is successfully completed.
TCCNS Equivalent: CHEM 2325
Additional Fee: Y
