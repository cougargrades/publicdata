HIST 2360 - Introduction to the History of Science, Medicine, and Technology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
This course introduces students to key concepts of the historical study of science, technology, and medicine from the ancient world to the present. It examines science, technology, and medicine as products of specific social and historical conditions, as well as what social changes they have induced throughout history.
