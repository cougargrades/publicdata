HIST 4348 - Society and Culture in Early Modern England, 1500-1700 - Capstone

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Students must have completed one of the following: HIST 2303 , HIST 2311 , HIST 2312 , HIST 2322 , HIST 2313 , or HIST 3353 .
Description
Undergraduate seminar centering on social and cultural patterns and change in England in the 1500s and 1600s, a time of dramatic socio-economic and cultural transformation. Topics include popular culture, religious change, gender and patriarchy, law and order, magic and witchcraft. Course Notes: This is a Capstone Course and satisfies the degree requirement for a B.A. in History.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
