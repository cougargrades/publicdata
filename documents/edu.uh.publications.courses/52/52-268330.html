CHEM 4369 - Analytical Chemistry 2

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 3119 and CHEM 3369 , or equivalent.
Description
Theory of optical, electroanalytical, and chromatographic methods of analysis, including electronic aspects of modern instrumentation.
Note: May not apply toward degree until CHEM 4229 is successfully completed.
