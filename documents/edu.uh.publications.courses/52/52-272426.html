ARTS 4390 -  Museum Internship at Blaffer Art Museum

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.
Description
The internship gives student an active, involved role in learning about practices of Blaffer Art Museum.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students with an interest in pursuing careers in the art museum field complete projects that teach them about contemporary art and art history while providing practicum. Each student is assigned to one or more department (such as curatorial, education). A series of assignments concludes with a final written assignment and presentation.
Additional Fee: N
