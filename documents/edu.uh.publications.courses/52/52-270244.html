MECE 3336 - Mechanics II (Dynamics)

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331 , ENGR 2301 or MECE 2336 , MATH 2415 .
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3321 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Kinematics and dynamics of particles and rigid bodies, and introduction to mechanical vibrations.
Repeatability: No
