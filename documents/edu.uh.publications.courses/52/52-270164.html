MATH 1300 - Fundamentals of Math

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
A survey of precollege algebra. Topics include signed numbers, operations with algebraic expressions, first-degree equations and inequalities in one variable, factoring, the Cartesian coordinate system, systems of first-degree equations in two variables solved by graphical and algebraic means, exponents and radicals, an introduction to quadratic equations, and stated problems.
Repeatability: No

Note: This course may not be used to satisfy any degree requirements. This course is intended for students who have not attained a satisfactory college readiness score in mathematics.
Additional Fee: Y
