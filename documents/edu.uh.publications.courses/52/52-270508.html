PETR 3362 -  Reservoir Engineering I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2415 and 3321, and PETR 2313 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Rock and fluid properties, P-V-T behavior of crude oil and natural gas, fundamentals of fluid flow through porous media, reservoir energy.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
