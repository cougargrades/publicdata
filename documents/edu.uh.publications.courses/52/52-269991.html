ITAL 2102 - Interm Ital Converversation II

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ITAL 1302 , ITAL 1502 , or ITAL 2311 , or consent of instructor.
Corequisite: May be taken concurrently with ITAL 2311 or ITAL 2312 .
Description
Intensive practice in spoken Italian.
Repeatability: May be repeated for credit with consent of instructor.

Note: Cannot be used to satisfy 2000-level language requirement.
