BIOE 5341 - Advanced Biofluid Dynamics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 3340, and BIOE 4302 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Mathematical modeling, computational methods and simulation of fluid flow and mass transport in biological context, with applications in human physiology.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
