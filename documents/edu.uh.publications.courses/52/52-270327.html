MECT 4341 - Materials Selection & Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Grade of C- or better in MECT 3365 and MECT 4372 .
Description
Material selection as part of the design process; material properties, process and process selection, hybrid materials, and case studies.
Repeatability: No
