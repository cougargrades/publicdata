MATH 3363 -  Introduction to Partial Differential Equations

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: MATH 2415 and either MATH 3321 or MATH 3331 .
Description
Partial differential equations and boundary value problems, Fourier series, the heat equation, vibrations of continuous systems, the potential equation, spectral methods.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students may not receive credit for both MATH 3363 and MATH 4370.
Additional Fee: Yes
