ENGL 2350 - Writing the Global City

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
This is an intermediate writing course which examines the role that writing plays in the global city. The course will both study and analyze writers whose work highlights the complexities of their lived experiences in global cities.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: No
