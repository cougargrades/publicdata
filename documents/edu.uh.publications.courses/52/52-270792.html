PSYC 4307 -  Research Practica

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Intensive experience on a research project which culminates in the production of a scholarly effort such as a professional article or presentation.
Repeatability: May be repeated once for credit.

Additional Fee: Yes
