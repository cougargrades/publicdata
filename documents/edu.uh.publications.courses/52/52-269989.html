ITAL 1601 - Accelerated Italian

Credit Hours: 6
Lecture Contact Hours: 6 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Accelerated grammar course covering ITAL 1501 and ITAL 1502 . Geared toward Spanish-speaking students. Taught in Italian.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students can take ITAL 1601 after taking ITAL 1501. After completing ITAL 1601, students cannot take ITAL 1502.
Additional Fee: Y
