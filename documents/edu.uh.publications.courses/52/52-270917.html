SOC 3373 -  Comparative Family Structures

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
Historical and cross-national variations, with attention to differences between U.S. racial/ethnic groups and the impact of industrialization upon family changes.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
