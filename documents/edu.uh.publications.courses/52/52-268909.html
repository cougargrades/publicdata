ECE 3317 -  Applied Electromagnetic Waves

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 2126 and PHYS 2326 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in ECE 3337 .
Description
Maxwell’s equations in time and frequency domains, Poynting’s theorem, plane wave propagation, reflection and transmission in lossless and lossy media, transmission lines, waveguides, and antennas.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
