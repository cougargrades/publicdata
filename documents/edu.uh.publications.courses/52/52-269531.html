HIST 2382 -  African American History since 1865

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Examination of the experience and contributions of Black people in the history and development of America since 1865, including emancipation, race relations, urbanization, war, politics, economics, and civil rights.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (60) Core-American History
Additional Fee: Yes
