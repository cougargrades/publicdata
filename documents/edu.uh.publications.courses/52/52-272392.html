NURS 3312 - Fundamental Concepts of Care

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 45 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: NURS 3310 , NURS 3311 , NURS 3315 , NURS 4521 .
Corequisite: NURS 3313 Fundamental Concepts Clinical. Credit for or Concurrent Enrollment in: NURS 3314 Pharmacology.
Description
This course focuses on utilization of the nursing process as a systematic approach to provide safe, evidenced based foundational care to adult patient.
Additional Fee: N
