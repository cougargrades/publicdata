INAR 3360 -  Human Factors in Interior Architecture

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INAR 2501 .
Description
Survey of theory and case studies in human factors engineering (anthropometry, ergonomics), environmental behavior (proxemics) and psychology, perception, color, light and acoustics’ effects on occupant health safety, welfare, and experience.
