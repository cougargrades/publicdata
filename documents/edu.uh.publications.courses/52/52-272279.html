NURS 4220 -  Clinical Decision Making

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: NURS 3631 & NURS 3633 or NURS 3312 & NURS 3313
Description
Review of fundamental concepts of nursing care required for licensure examination and entry into the practice of professional nursing. Includes assessment of cognitive deficits, planning and implementation of needed remediation.
