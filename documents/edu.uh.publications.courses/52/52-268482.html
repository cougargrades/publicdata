COMM 4310 -  Opinion Writing & Blogging

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 3314 or COMM 3316 .
Description
Preparing elements of editorial page including research and writing of commentaries, columns, and editorials. In addition learning how to create, write and maintain an online blog.
