MUTX 3131 -  Music Therapy Practicum I

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MUTX 2301 and acceptance into Music Therapy major.
Corequisite: MUTX 3331 .
Description
Students attend weekly Music Therapy group sessions and assist with documentation and data collection, with a focus on observation and measurement skills. This contributes to the overall total of 1200 clinical hours required for certification.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
