MECE 3363 -  Introduction To Fluid Mechanics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 2334 , 3336, and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3363 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Fluid properties, control volume and differential balance equations, viscous and irrotational flows, dimensional analysis, laminar and turbulent flows.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
