PSYC 3338 -  Psychology of Older Adults

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Lifespan, research-based perspective of the biopsychosocial stages of older adulthood, age 55 and over.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
