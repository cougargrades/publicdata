SPEC 4362 -  Behavior: Evidence-Based Decisions

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollement in EDUC 2301 .
Description
Discusses individualized behavior-analytic strategies such as behavioral observation, assessment, data collection tools, and evidence-based intervention decisions that teachers can implement with learners who emit challenging behaviors across a variety of settings within a multi-tiered system of support framework.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
