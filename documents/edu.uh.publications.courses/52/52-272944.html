PSYC 4388 -  Latino Health and Disparities

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Lifespan, research-based perspectives of the multi-path (biological, psychological, social, ethnic) determinants of health and health disparities in Latina/o/x communities in the United States.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
