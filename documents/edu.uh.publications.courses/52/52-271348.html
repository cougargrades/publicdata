SPEC 4353 - Assistive Technology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Formerly EPSY 4353 Technology in Special Populations
Prerequisite: EDUC 2301 .
Description
Knowledge and skills of professionals working in special education to support students with special needs with classroom technology and assistive technology, including state and federal laws and regulations related to assistive technology.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
