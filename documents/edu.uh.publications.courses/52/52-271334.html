ARCH 2328 -  Technology 2

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ARCH 2327 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in ARCH 2501 or INAR 2501 .
Description
Introductory investigation and analysis of structural systems and their materials, climate/human environment responsive systems selection, and life safety systems.
Additional Fee: Y
