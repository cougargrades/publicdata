MECE 4364 - Heat Transfer

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3363 and MECE 3363 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Steady and unsteady heat conduction; heat transfer by forced and free convection, radiation, and phase change; numerical solutions, and heat transfer system synthesis.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
