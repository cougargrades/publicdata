GERM 3330 - Reading German Texts

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GERM 2312 or equivalent.
Description
Textual analysis with selected fictional and nonfictional readings in German. Development of reading and writing skills.
Repeatability: No

Note: May be taken concurrently with GERM 3333 .
Additional Fee: Y
