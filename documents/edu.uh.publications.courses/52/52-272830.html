MAS 4300 - Capstone Project in Latino/a Studies

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MAS 3344 .
Description
Application of the theoretical knowledge acquired during the course of the Major and concentration track to either an academic or industry, governmental or non-governmental project. The research project will reflect the area of study of interest to the student and add to the body of knowledge on that topic.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
