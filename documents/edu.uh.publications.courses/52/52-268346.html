CHIN 1502 -  Elementary Chinese II

Credit Hours: 5
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: Completion of CHIN 1501 with a minimum grade of C- in twelve months immediately prior to enrollment or placement by examination immediately prior to enrollment.
Description
Introduction to modern spoken and written Mandarin Chinese.
Repeatability: No

Note: Restricted to nonnative speakers of Chinese.
TCCNS Equivalent: CHIN 1512
Additional Fee: Y
