CHEE 5370 -  Sustainability and Environmental Mitigation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEE 3466 , or CHEM 4370 (or equivalent) and approval by the instructor; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
This course addresses sustainability and environmental mitigation in the context of chemical process understanding. This includes analysis of the impact of various energy-related factors on the environment (e.g. carbon footprints, pollutant release, renewable resources and energy conservation), and methodologies to mitigate these impacts.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
