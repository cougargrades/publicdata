CNE 4225 - Utility System Construction

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CNE 3310 , CNE 3311 .
Description
Materials, methods and techniques associated with construction of major utility systems, including water, sewer, communications, natural gas and electrical.
Repeatability: Yes
