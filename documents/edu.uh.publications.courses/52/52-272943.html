POPH 2300 - Introduction to Population Health

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
An introduction to how the multiple determinants of health (e.g., health care, socioeconomic status, genetics, the physical environment and health behavior, and their interactions) have implications for the health outcomes of populations.
Repeatability: No

Note: If taking this course towards the Minor in Population Health, contact the Population Health Advisor to declare the minor. A cumulative 2.0 minimum GPA is required for minor approval. A minimum grade of C in this course is required to advance in the Population Health Minor.
Additional Fee: No
