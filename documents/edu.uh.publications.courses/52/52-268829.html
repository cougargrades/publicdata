ECON 3332 -  Intermediate Microeconomics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ECON 2302 and either MATH 1325 or MATH 2413 , or Math major with sophomore standing in the Option in Mathematical Finance program.
Description
Rigorous introduction to resource allocation in a market economy including consumer behavior, firm behavior, supply and demand, efficiency and the determination of prices.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
