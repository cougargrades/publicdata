ARTS 4304 -  Senior Painting Major II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ARTS 4300 - ARTS 4301 - ARTS 4302 and review of portfolio by major faculty.
Corequisite: Concurrent enrollment in ARTS 4303 , ARTS 4304, ARTS 4305 required.
Description
Continued concentrated studio immersion emphasizing conceptual and critical skills to develop a unique, cohesive, pertinent, and timely body of work. Ongoing critical studies, faculty mentoring, critiques, and visiting artists.
Repeatability: Yes

Note: ARTS 4303 , ARTS 4304, and ARTS 4305 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
Additional Fee: Y
