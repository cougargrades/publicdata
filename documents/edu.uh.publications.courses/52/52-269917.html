INDE 4388 -  Engineering Leadership and Entrepreneurism

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 2304 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Entrepreneurism, business planning, cost classifications, accounting and management information systems, finance, cash management, human resources and culture, ethics, and team building.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
