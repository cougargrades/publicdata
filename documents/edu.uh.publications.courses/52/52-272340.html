COSC 4371 -  Security Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: A grade of C- or better in MATH 2318 , MATH 3339 , and COSC 3371 .
Description
Advanced techniques such as deep learning, Adversarial Machine Learning, online and imbalanced learning. Semester long project on real-world cybersecurity data.
