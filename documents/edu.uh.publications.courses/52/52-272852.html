MUTX 4333 -  Music Therapy Techniques III: Medical Music Therapy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUTX 3332 .
Corequisite: MUTX 4133 .
Description
With a focus on neurologic and physiologic responses to music, students will gain perspective of the use of music therapy for medical populations, and will learn medical music therapy treatment protocols.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
