MECE 5320 - Intro Nanomaterials Engineering

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECE 5319 or CHEE 5319 , concurrent enrollment in MECE 5120 , or consent of instructor; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Engineering of nanomaterials with emphasis on structural, optical, photonic, magnetic, and electronic materials. Synthetic methods and analytical characterization with design for applications will be emphasized.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
