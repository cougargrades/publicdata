SPAN 2307 - Spanish for Heritage Learners I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of SPAN 1507 with a minimum of C- or placement exam.
Description
Basic skills in reading, spelling, and composition.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit for both SPAN 2307 and SPAN 2311 will not apply to a degree. Designed for students from a Spanish-speaking background.
Additional Fee: Y
