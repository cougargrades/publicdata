CHEE 2332 -  Chem Engr Thermodyn I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 1312 , MATH 2415 , PHYS 2325 , and CHEE 2331 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Fundamental concepts of thermodynamic systems, heat and work, properties of pure substances, first and second laws.
