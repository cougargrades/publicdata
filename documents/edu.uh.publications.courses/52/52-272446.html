CEA 4422 -  Communications and Networking

Credit Hours: 4
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: CEA 3311 , INDE 2333 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Computer communication technologies, networking models and standards, local area networks, wide area networks, low-power communications and personal area networks, wired and wireless network protocols, emerging trends and applications.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
