HIST 2314 - Global Civilization since 1500

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
A global overview of the interactions among major cultural traditions (Judeo-Christian, Graeco-Roman, Indian, Chinese, African, Islamic, Indigenous) from 1500 CE.
