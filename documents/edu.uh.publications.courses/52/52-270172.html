MATH 2413 -  Calculus I

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Credit for MATH 2312 with a grade of C- or higher, or a satisfactory score on a placement examination.
Description
Calculus of rational functions, limits, derivatives, applications of the derivative, antiderivatives, the definite integral with applications, mean value theorem, fundamental theorem of calculus, and numerical integration.
Core Category: (90) Core-Math/Reasoning, Component Area Option (20) Core-Mathematics
TCCNS Equivalent: MATH 2413
Additional Fee: Y
