SPEC 4375 - Ethics in Applied Behavior Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPEC 3372 and SPEC 3373 .
Description
Discusses professional issues such as ethical principles, ethics codes and requirements, and professionalism utilized in Applied Behavior Analysis. Diversity, equity, and inclusion are addressed.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
