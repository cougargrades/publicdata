CHEM 3119 - Anlytical Chemistry Lab

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 4
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in CHEM 3369 .
Description
Laboratory methods in analytical chemistry, including quantitative separation techniques, electrochemistry, and absorption spectroscopy.
Repeatability: No

Note: For science majors only. May not apply toward degree until CHEM 3369 is successfully completed.
Additional Fee: Y
