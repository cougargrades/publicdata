PETR 2311 - Reservoir Petrophysics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331 , MATH 2414 , PHYS 2325 .
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 2415 , PETR 1111 , PETR 2111 and PHYS 2326 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Systematic theoretical and laboratory study of lithology, porosity, effective permeability, relative permeability, fluid saturations, capillary characteristics, compressibility, rock stress and rock-fluid interactions.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
