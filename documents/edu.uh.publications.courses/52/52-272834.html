MUSI 3123 - Jazz Improvisation III: Advanced Jazz Concepts

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 2122 and successful completion of jazz performance barrier.
Description
The third of three sequential courses. Focuses on the application of jazz theory to advanced improvisation. Students explore selected jazz repertoire in depth and apply technical and theoretical concepts.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
