MUSI 1117 -  Aural Skills II

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MUSI 1116 and credit for or enrollment in MUSI 1312 .
Description
Continuation of MUSI 1116 , through modal mixture and diatonic sevenths; compound meter; decorative chromaticism; alto clef.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: MUSI 1117
Additional Fee: Y
