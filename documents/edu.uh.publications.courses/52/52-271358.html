BIOE 5318 - Introduction to Biomedical Informatics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 3341 or BIOE 3440 or consent of instructor; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Theoretical foundations and the current applications of biomedical informatics in health sciences and health care delivery systems.
Repeatability: No
