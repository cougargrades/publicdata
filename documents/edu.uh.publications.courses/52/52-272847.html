MUTX 3332 - Music Therapy Techniques II: Early Childhood and Special Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUTX 3331 .
Corequisite: MUTX 3132 .
Description
Students will gain a basic understanding of how to use musical interventions and build therapeutic relationships with children and adolescents to address development, physical, cognitive and social emotional needs.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
