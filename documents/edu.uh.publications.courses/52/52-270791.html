PSYC 4306 -  Community Service Practica

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Direct experience in the application of psychological theory to a community service program.
Repeatability: May be repeated once for credit.

Additional Fee: Yes
