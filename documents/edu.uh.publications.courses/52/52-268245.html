CHEE 3363 -  Fluid Mechanics for Chemical Engineers

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEE 2332 and CHEE 3321 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Foundations of fluid mechanics, fluid statics, kinematics, laminar and turbulent flow; macroscopic balances; dimensional analysis and flow correlations.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
