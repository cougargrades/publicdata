CHEE 5323 - Fundamental of Tissue Engineer

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEE 3363 or MECE 3363 or BIOE 3440 and CHEE 3321 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Fundamental concepts in tissue engineering and cell biology including structure, function, and replication.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be received for more than one of BIOE 4323, CHEE 5395, or MECE 5323.
Additional Fee: Yes
