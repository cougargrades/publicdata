CHEE 5368 -  Chemical Process Economics I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Senior standing and concurrent enrollment in CHEE 4321 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Managerial economics of chemical processes and products; development of decision-making methods using chemical industry examples.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
