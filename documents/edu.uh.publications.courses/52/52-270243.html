MECE 4343 - Thermal Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 2334 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MECE 4364 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Power and refrigeration cycles, mixture of ideal gases, basic combustion processes, thermodynamics of compressible flow, thermal system design.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
