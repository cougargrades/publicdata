THEA 4345 -  Dramaturgy Practicum

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: THEA 2343 and consent of instructor.
Description
Study and practical application serving as the dramaturg or co-dramaturg for a production in the UH Theatre and Dance season.
