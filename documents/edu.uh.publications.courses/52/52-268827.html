ECON 2301 -  Principles of Macroeconomics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1314 or equivalent or consent of instructor.
Description
Aggregate economy, inflation, recession, and economic policy. Economic growth, business cycle theory, and international trade.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be earned in both ECON 2301 and 2305. Economics majors/minors with at least a B+ in ECON 2301 may petition to substitute 2301 for 2305.
Core Category: (80) Core-Social & Behavioral Science
TCCNS Equivalent: ECON 2301
Additional Fee: Y
