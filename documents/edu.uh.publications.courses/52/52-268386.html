CIVE 4312 -  Civil Engineering Capstone Design Project

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: CIVE 3332 , CIVE 4311 , CIVE 4332 , CIVE 4333 , CIVE 4363 , CIVE 4369 and INDE 2333 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Capstone design project. Includes constraints such as economic, environmental, social, political, ethical, health and safety, constructability, and sustainability.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
