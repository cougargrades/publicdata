INAR 4211 - Advanced Topics in Design History

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Cross-Listed As: Cross-list as: ARCH 4211 and INDS 4211.

Prerequisite: Total of 3 credits or more from following courses: INAR 3111 , INAR 3211 , INAR 3311 , ARCH 3341 , ARCH 3342 , ARCH 3343 , ARCH 3347 , ARCH 3354 , ARCH 3371 , ARCH 3380 .
Description
Two-credit module focused on an advanced selected topic in the history, theory, and criticism of the human-impacted environment.
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: No
