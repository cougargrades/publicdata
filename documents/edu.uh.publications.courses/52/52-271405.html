MUSI 4370 -  Art Song Repertoire

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 3364 .
Description
Introduction to song repertoire includes fusion of poetry and music, composers’ song styles and signature musical characteristics grouped by national categories including German, French, American, British, Italian, Spanish, and Russian.
Note: Not an advanced literature elective.
