SPAN 3342 - Cross-Cultural Business Contexts: U.S./Latin America

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 3301 or SPAN 3307 , and SPAN 3302 or SPAN 3308 .
Description
Cultural values, attitudes, and perceptions that affect U.S./Latin American business negotiations and operations.
