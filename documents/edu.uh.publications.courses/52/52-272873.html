HIST 2357 -  South Asia and the British Empire

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Between the middle of the eighteenth century to the mid-nineteenth century, South Asia, now comprising several sovereign nations, was a part of the British Empire. Imperial rule in South Asia shaped Indian society and decisively influenced its economy. This course will help to situate key transformations in modern societies in South Asia.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (40) Core-Language, Philosophy & Culture
