SPAN 3311 - Advanced Composition and Creative Writing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 3302 or SPAN 3308 or by placement exam within a semester prior to enrollment.
Description
Intensive practice in advanced Spanish writing in a variety of formal and informal styles. Writing production of most common types, from reports to essays to creative works.
