MIS 4372 -  Transaction Processing Systems II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly DICS 4371
Prerequisite: MIS 3371 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Development and implementation of transaction processing systems with emphasis on operating systems and programming considerations.
