CIVE 4337 -  Transportation Engineering

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INDE 2333 and two CIVE 3000 level courses; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Introduction to transportation engineering emphasizing highway engineering, including geometric design, traffic engineering, transportation planning, and safety.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Engineering Equipment Fee
