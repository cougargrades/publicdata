BIOE 2331 -  Biomedical Processes

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331 , PHYS 2326 , CHEM 1312 , & MATH 2415 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3321 .
Description
Introduction to fundamental calculations, measurements, and processes encountered across the field of biomedical engineering.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
