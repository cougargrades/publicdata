TLIM 3360 -  Law and Ethics in Technology & Innovation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Grade of C- or better in TLIM 3340 or MANA 3335 .
Description
General principles of business law and ethics related to contracts, property, agency, corporations, bankruptcy, mortgages, torts, and the Uniform Commercial Code with emphasis on technology and innovation.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
