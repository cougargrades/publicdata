MATH 1342 -  Elementary Statistical Methods

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Probability, correct probabilistic reasoning, distributions, graphical and descriptive methods, sampling estimation, hypotheses and statistical inference.
Repeatability: No

Note: May not apply to course or GPA requirements for a major or minor in the College of Natural Sciences and Mathematics. Students with prior credit for MATH 3338 or MATH 3339 may not enroll in or receive credit for MATH 1342.
Core Category: (90) Core-Math/Reasoning, Component Area Option
(20) Core-Mathematics
TCCNS Equivalent: MATH 1342
Additional Fee: Y
