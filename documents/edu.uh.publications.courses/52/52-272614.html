COMM 3381 -  Short Film Practicum

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.
Description
A three-week, hands-on, intense exercise in short film production. This course is for students interested in and passionate about film production. May be taken twice for credit when genre differs.
Repeatability: Yes

Note: Enrollment is by consent of instructor only. Students will be assigned crew positions based on experience and skill level.
Additional Fee: Y
