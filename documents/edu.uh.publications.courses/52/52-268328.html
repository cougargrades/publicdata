CHEM 4364 - Advanced Organic Chem

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 2125 , CHEM 2325 , and credit for or concurrent enrollment in CHEM 4372 .
Description
Applications of physical principles of organic chemistry, including study of reaction mechanisms, structure determination, and design in syntheses.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
