PSYC 4308 -  Psychopharmacology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 , and PSYC 2330 .
Description
This course covers chemical transmitters and their modulation by drugs and medications, within the context of mental health. Beginning with the simpler chemical transmitters and progressing through the more complex, the course will cover drugs that target them, as well as relevant brain disease states.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
