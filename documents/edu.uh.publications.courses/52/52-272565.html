SOC 3341 - Organizations

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 .
Description
This course surveys the sociology literature on formal organizations, thereby introducing students to both the internal workings of organizations and the external pressures that act on them. Particular attention will be paid to the underlying motives of organization, the different types of organization, and the role of organizations in society.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
