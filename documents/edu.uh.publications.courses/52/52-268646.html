COSC 4393 -  Digital Image Processing

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: A grade of C- or better in COSC 2320 or COSC 2436 .
Description
Essential concepts of digital image processing, image acquisition and processing, practical applications, and elementary image analysis algorithms.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
