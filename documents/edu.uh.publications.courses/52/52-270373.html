MUSI 1120 - University Chorus

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None; no audition required.
Description
Choruses that are open to all singers, whether novice or experienced. Section 1: This chorus consists of soprano and alto voices only. Section 2: This chorus consists of tenor and bass voices only.
Repeatability: May be repeated for credit.

Note: May be repeated for credit.
