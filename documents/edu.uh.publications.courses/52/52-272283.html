SOC 3395 -  Careers in Sociology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 .
Description
Using a sociological perspective, the course explores careers available to students with a Bachelor’s degree in Sociology; we accomplish this through readings, guest speakers, and research about careers in the private/for-profit sector, nonprofit/foundations, public sector (city, county, state, and federal government), and academia (including graduate school).
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: N
