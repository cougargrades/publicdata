COMM 3382 -  Television II and Sports Production

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: COMM 2322 .
Description
Continuation of instruction on the tools and techniques of studio television and sports productions. The aesthetics of media production, especially in a studio environment, is stressed. Hands-on instruction in the television studio and production control room continues students’ learning and performance of duties as Producers, Directors, Stage Managers, Camera Crew, Audio Operators, Lightning Crew, Control Room Crew and Grips both in television and sports production.
Additional Fee: Y
