DANC 2107 -  Flamenco Dance

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: None.
Description
This is an open-level dance course that focuses on traditional Spanish Flamenco dance styles. Emphasis will be placed on fluidity of the arms, rhythmic footwork, and personal expression.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
