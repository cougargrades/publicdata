MECT 1365 - Elements of Materials and Processes

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Survey of manufacturing processes. Application of machine tools, measuring tools, and primary processes to the manufacturing cycle. May not apply to the Mechanical Engineering Technology Degree.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students may not receive credit for MECT 1364 and MECT 1365.
Additional Fee: No
