INAR 4500 -  Interior Architecture Design Studio VII

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: ARCH 2350 , 2351, and INAR 3501 .
Description
Large scale building interior design factors, including programming, and the human and environmental impact of individual design applications in the built environment.
