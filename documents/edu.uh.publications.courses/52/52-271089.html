DRAM 2331 -  Lighting and Sound Technology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: Acceptance to the THEA or DAN major by audition/portfolio review and consent of instructor.
Description
Introduction to equipment, tools, and techniques used in theatrical lighting and sound.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: DRAM 2331
Additional Fee: Y
