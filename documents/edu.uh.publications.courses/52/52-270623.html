PHYS 2326 -  University Physics II (lecture)

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: PHYS 2325
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 2415 .
Description
Second semester of a two part calculus-based course covering thermodynamics, electricity, magnetism, electromagnetic waves, optics, and modern physics.
Repeatability: No

Note: Primarily for science and engineering majors. Credit may not be applied toward degree for both PHYS 2326 and PHYS 1302 .
Core Category: (30) Core-Life & Physical Sciences
TCCNS Equivalent: PHYS 2326
Additional Fee: Y
