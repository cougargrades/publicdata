CHEM 1111 -  Fundamentals of Chm Lab

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in CHEM 1311.
Description
Illustrates and reinforces principles and concepts by use of qualitative and quantitative experiments, emphasizing interpretation and reporting of data and facility in handling scientific instruments.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be applied toward a degree for both CHEM 1111 and CHEM 1105 or CHEM 1117.
TCCNS Equivalent: CHEM 1111
Additional Fee: Y
