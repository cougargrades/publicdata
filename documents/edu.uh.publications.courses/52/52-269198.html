FINA 4334 -  Managerial Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly FINA 3334
Prerequisite: ACCT 2302 , MATH 1324 , MATH 1325 , and ECON 2302 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Allocation of resources within the business enterprise. Emphasis on profit maximization in the context of alternative market structures.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
