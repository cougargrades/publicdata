POLS 4353 -  Contemporary Issues in Law, Politics, and Society

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2305 and GOVT 2306 or equivalent.
Description
This course analyzes topics such as free expression, political protests, political representation and voting rights using theories and methodologies of social science.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
