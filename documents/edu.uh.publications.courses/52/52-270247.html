MECE 3360 - Experimental Methods

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331 , ENGI 2304 , MECE 2334 , MECE 3336 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Sensors, transducers, signal conditioning, data acquisition and data analysis. Measurement of length, strain, force, temperature, pressure, and velocity.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
