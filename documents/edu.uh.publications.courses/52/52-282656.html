HIST 3374 -  Ottoman Empire II (1566-1923)

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: HIST 2312 OR HIST 2314 OR HIST 2322 .
Description
History of the Ottoman Empire from the conquest of Istanbul in 1453 until the end of World War I; includes analysis of the concepts such as decline, early modernity, and nationalism.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
