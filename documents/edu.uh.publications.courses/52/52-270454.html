MUSI 4365 - Contemporary Concert Music

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 2362 for music composition majors. MUSI 3364 for all other majors.
Description
Examines concert music composers, works and organizations of the last 50 years.
Repeatability: No
