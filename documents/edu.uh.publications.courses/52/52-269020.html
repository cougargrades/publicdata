ELED 4310 -  Reading and Language Arts in the Elementary School

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: admission to QUEST 2 and six semester hours of sophomore level English.
Description
Field-based course that introduces curriculum, concepts, methods, and materials for teaching reading and language arts in the elementary school.
