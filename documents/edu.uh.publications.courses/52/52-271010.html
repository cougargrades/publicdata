SCLT 3385 -  Transport Economics and Policy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 2302 and C or better in SCLT 2362 .
Description
Cost structures and service capabilities of the major modes of transportation. History and effects of regulatory policies of carriers and shippers.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
