GEOL 4386 - Petroleum Geoscience for Petroleum Engineers

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GEOL 1303 , GEOL 1103 , PETR 2313 , and declared major in Petroleum Engineering.
Description
Formation and distribution of conventional and unconventional petroleum resources, and the geological and geophysical methods used in their exploration, characterization, and production.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
