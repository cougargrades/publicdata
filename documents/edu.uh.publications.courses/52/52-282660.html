HIST 4364 - The History of Pan African Nationalism in Latin America and the Caribbean

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: HIST 2371 or HIST 2372 or HIST 3376 .
Description
This course explores how Black intellectuals, literary figures, artists, social and labor activists and movements developed ideologies and discourses of empowerment to contest the marginalization and exploitation of people of African descent in Latin America and the Caribbean during the 20th century.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
