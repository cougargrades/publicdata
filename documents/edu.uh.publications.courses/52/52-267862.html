ARAB 3340 -  Modern & Rationalism in Islamic Tradition

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Systematic analysis of classical Islamic philosophical discourse and its contribution to the development of Islamic Modernism and Arab Enlightenment: including the Qur’an, Ghazali, Averroes, Muhammad Abduh, and Nasr Abu Zayd.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taught in English.
Core Category: (40) Core-Language, Philosophy & Culture
Additional Fee: Y
