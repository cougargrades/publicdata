CHEM 4336 -  Fundamentl Biochemistry

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 2125 , CHEM 2325 , and CHEM 4372 .
Description
Organic and physical chemistry of biological molecules, metabolic pathways, and control mechanisms; organization of living cells.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be received for both CHEM 4336 and BCHS 3304 or BCHS 3305 .
Additional Fee: Y
