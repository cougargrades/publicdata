RELS 3330 - Christianity

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Christianity from the post-biblical era to the present, including church fathers, heresies, medieval Christian philosophy, Greek and Latin churches, the reformation, modern and American Christianity.
