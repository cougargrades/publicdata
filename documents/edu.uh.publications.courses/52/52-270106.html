MANA 4350 - International Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 , or equivalent; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Students may not receive credit for both MANA 4350 and INTB 4350. Managerial effectiveness and its implications for global organizations.
