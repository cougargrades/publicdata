COSC 4348 - Introduction to Game Art and Animation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: A grade of C- or better in COSC 2320 or COSC 2436 .
Description
Video game concept arts and computer animation. Modeling game assets and animation using the latest game software and hardware.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
