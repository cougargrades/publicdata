SOC 3363 - Sociology of Poverty

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
Sociological perspectives on poverty and income inequality using an intersectional lens exploring poverty in social institutions, social policies, stereotypes, and social relationships.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
