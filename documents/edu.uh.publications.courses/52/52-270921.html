SOC 3385 -  Sociology of World Religions

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
Knowledge of the world’s religious systems, social-structural factors associated with their origins and development and the impact on contemporary societies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
