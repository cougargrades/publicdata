PETR 3363 -  Fluid Mechanics for Petroleum Engineers

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INDE 2333 , MATH 3321 , MECE 2334 , Petroleum Engineering Major; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Foundations of petroleum fluid mechanics, fluid statics, kinematics, laminar and turbulent flow; macroscopic balances; dimensional analysis and flow correlations. Radial flow into a wellbore.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
