ENGI 1331 - Computing and Problem Solving for Engineers

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100 , MATH 2413 and CHEM 1311 OR PHYS 2325 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Engineering undergraduate standing or permission of the Director of Undergraduate Programs in Engineering. Introduction to computing; matrix arithmetic, programming constructs, algorithms and graphical visualization using MATLAB; problem solving applications in engineering analysis and design.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
