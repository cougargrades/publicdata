SPEC 4367 -  Strategies for Collaboration, Coaching, and Consultation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Formerly EPSY 4367 Consultation Strategies and Coaching
Prerequisite: EDUC 2301 .
Description
Discusses theories and strategies to prepare special education personnel, classroom teachers, school administrators, paraeducators, related service personnel and other support staff for engaging in collaborative school consultation and teamwork in serving the educational needs of diverse populations.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
