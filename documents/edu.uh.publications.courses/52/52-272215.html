RELS 3373 - Jews in the Ancient World

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Overview of Jewish history from the Hellenistic period through the rise of the rabbis (c. 4th century B.C.E.-4th century C.E.). The Maccabean Revolt and Hasmonean Dynasty, Herodian Dynasty, Dead Sea Scrolls, sectarianism, the rise of Christianity, and the destruction of Jerusalem in 70 C.E through Jewish literature produced during this period: biblical materials, Apocrypha, Pseudepigrapha, and rabbinic texts.
