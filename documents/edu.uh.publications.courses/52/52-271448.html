ARTH 3326 -  European Romanticism

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ARTS 1303 and ARTS 1304 , or consent of instructor.
Description
French, Spanish, British and German Romanticism with special attention on the political context including the rise of the Napoleonic Empire, the conservation Restoration after its fall and resistance to both.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
