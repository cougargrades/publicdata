SPAN 3331 - Mexican American Literature

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Literature in Spanish language produced by people of Mexican origin in what is today the United States from the Colonial Period to the present.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taught in Spanish.
Core Category: (40) Core-Language, Philosophy & Culture
Additional Fee: Y
