MATH 3330 - Abstract Algebra

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2318 and MATH 3325 .
Description
An introduction to groups and rings: groups, subgroups, quotients, products, homomorphisms and isomorphisms, ideals, integral domains and division rings.
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: Y
