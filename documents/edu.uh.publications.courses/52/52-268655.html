CNST 1330 - Graphics I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Lettering, geometric construction, multiview and auxiliary projections, sections, and connections, dimensioning, and isometric and oblique pictorials. Manual and computer aided drawing techniques.
