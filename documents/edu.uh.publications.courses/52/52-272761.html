GHL 2155 - Event Implementation

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Students will assist in recruiting volunteers for special events of the college. Responsibilities include attending training sessions, orientation sessions, setup and breakdown shifts, and attending the event.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
