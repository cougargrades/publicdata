POLS 3363 -  Lobbying in Theory and Practice

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2305 and GOVT 2306 or equivalent.
Description
An understanding of the fundamentals of lobbying from both a theoretical and practical perspective by covering the history of the lobbying industry, strategies of lobbyists, and case studies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
