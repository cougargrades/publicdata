PSYC 4305 -  Persuasion & Behavior

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Social psychological theory analysis and application to social communication, self concept, attitude and behavior change, and information processing.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
