THEA 3375 -  Costume Technology & Applied Crafts

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: THEA 2334 , THEA 2373 , and consent of instructor.
Description
Study and use of craft skills, materials, and techniques used in costume construction, millinery, and properties.
