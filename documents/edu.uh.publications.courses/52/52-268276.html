CHEE 5377 -  Intro To Polymer Science

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEE 3363 and either 3366 or CHEE 3466 or consent of instructor; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Synthesis, characterization, physical properties and processing of polymeric materials. Methods to measure and characterize the correlations among structure processing properties of polymeric materials.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
