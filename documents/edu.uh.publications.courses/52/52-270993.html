SPAN 4396 - Topics in Spanish Language and Linguistics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 3301 or SPAN 3307 and SPAN 3302 or SPAN 3308 .
Description
Topics in Spanish language, dialects, and linguistic theory.
Repeatability: May be repeated when topics vary.
