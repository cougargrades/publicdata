ENGL 4382 -  Poetry Writing

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission to the Creative Writing Concentration and 3 hours in 3000-/or 4000-level courses in poetry.
Description
Analysis and writing of lyric poetry, including crafting of complete poems; traditional and contemporary lyrics; and lyric as a genre.
Repeatability: May be repeated once for credit.

Additional Fee: Y
