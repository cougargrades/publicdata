LASS 3150 -  CLASS Internship

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 1.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 and approval of the CLASS Dean’s Office.
Description
The course is part of an assigned work experience central to one of the College of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences internship programs in cooperation with supervisors in government or business. Course Notes: The course is part of an approved CLASS internship program administered by the Dean¿s Office. The internship programs are announced annually via email, social media, and the CLASS Dean’s Office Website.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
