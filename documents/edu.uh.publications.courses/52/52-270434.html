MUSI 4230 -  Instrumental Conducting I

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 2211 and MUSI 2214 .
Description
Fundamental aspects of gesture in instrumental conducting, hierarchical listening, ensemble sound production and pitch, collective breathing techniques, basic score study, and rehearsal planning and pacing.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
