AAS 3339 -  Aging and Health in Africa and the USA

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: AAS 2320 .
Description
An overview of the structural forces that lead to stratified later-life health and well-being outcomes among Africans in Africa and the USA.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
