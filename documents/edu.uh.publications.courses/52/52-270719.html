NSS 2342 - Introduction to National Security Studies

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
Introduction to intelligence and security needs, problems, and risk confronted by private and governmental organizations and national and homeland security.
Repeatability: No
