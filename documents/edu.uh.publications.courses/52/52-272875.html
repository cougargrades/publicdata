DIGM 3358 - Augmented and Virtual Reality

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: (Grade of C- or better in DIGM 1300 &nbsp;& DIGM 2325 ) or (CIS major and Grade of C- or better in CIS 2336 ).
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in DIGM 3354 or consent of instructor.
Description
Augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and mixed reality (MR) technologies including design and technological possibilities of AR/VR, media imaginations, economic implications, and related research.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
