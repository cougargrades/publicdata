MECE 5388 - Intelligent Structural Systems

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3345 , MECE 3338 , and senior standing in Mechanical Engineering; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Modeling, designing, and controlling intelligent structures using various smart materials such as piezoceramics, shape memory alloys, magnetorheological (MR) fluid, and fiber optical sensors.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
