ARTS 2366 - Watercolor

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: Twelve semester hours in foundation program studio courses (1000- level).
Description
Skill-based exploration of watercolor and its potential as an improvisational, expressive medium. Course will cover tools, media and supports / wet-on-wet, scumbling and dry brush techniques / lifting color, masking, glazing /basic color theory, value and composition.
Repeatability: Yes

TCCNS Equivalent: ARTS 2336
Additional Fee: Yes
