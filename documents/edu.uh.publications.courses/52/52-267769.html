INTN 4368 -  Comparative study of ASL and English Linguistics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INTN 3430 or permission of instructor.
Description
Linguistic analysis between spoken/written English and American Sign Language. Syntax, grammar, and other linguistic elements will be explored.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
