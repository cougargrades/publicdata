MUTX 2103 - Music Styles and Skills III

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MUTX 2102 .
Description
Piano and guitar skills for music therapists and music educators. Foundations for playing and harmonizing Hip-Hop, Rap, Dance and Electronic styles of music on both guitar and piano.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
