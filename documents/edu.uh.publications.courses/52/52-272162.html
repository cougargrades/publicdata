COMM 3313 -  Data-Driven Storytelling & Visualization

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1307 and either COMM 2311 or COMM 2328 .
Description
Students in this hands-on course will learn how to gather information, analyze it and visualize interactive, data-driven stories. Using database and visualization software, students will produce their own exciting digital stories and acquire skills highly valued in journalism jobs.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
