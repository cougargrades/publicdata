MIS 4379 -  MIS Consulting

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Good academic standing, and one of the following: pre-business major with junior status or above and with advisor approval, or a declared business major.
Description
Engagement in real world projects in organizations.
Repeatability: No

Note: The course is open to all majors in the Bauer College of Business.
Additional Fee: No
