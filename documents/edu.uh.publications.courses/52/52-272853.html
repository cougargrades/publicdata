MUTX 4334 - Music Therapy Techniques IV: Music Therapy in Behavioral Health

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUTX 4333 .
Corequisite: MUTX 4134 .
Description
Students will be introduced to theoretical and applied skills used in music therapy with psychiatric clients. Special attention will be paid to understanding the rationale for specific treatment approaches.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
