HON 3314 - Research and Writing in the Humanities

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or Equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
A seminar / workshop designed for undergraduates who want to develop skills and habits necessary for substantial research and writing in the traditional and interdisciplinary humanities.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
