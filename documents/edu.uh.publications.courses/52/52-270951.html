SPAN 3307 -  Public Speaking in Spanish

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 2308 or SPAN 3301 or placement by examination.
Description
Intensive practice in composition, strategy and delivery of speeches in Spanish. Recommended for students going into the professions, and for professionals who must use Spanish in formal and professional settings.
