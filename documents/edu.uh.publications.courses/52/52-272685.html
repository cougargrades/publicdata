BTIS 3323 -  Border Security and Cross-Border Challenges

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: 3 hours of Core Communication.
Description
Study of national border security and cross-border challenges, related to political, military, economic, trade and health matters, among others.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
