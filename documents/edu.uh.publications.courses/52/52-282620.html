MUSI 1150 -  Mariachi Ensemble

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 4
Prerequisite: Admission by audition only.
Description
Section 1: Mariachi Pumas An advanced ensemble open to students who excel at one of the following instruments: guitar, vihuela, guitarron, harp, violin, flute, accordion, marimba, and/or trumpet. Previous mariachi experience preferred, but not required. Section 2: Mariachi Universidad Open to all students who are proficient at one of following instruments: guitar, vihuela, guitarron, harp, violin, flute, accordion, marimba, and/or trumpet.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
