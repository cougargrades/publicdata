CHEE 2331 -  Chemical Processes

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 1312 , ENGI 1100 , ENGI 1331 , MATH 2414 or MATH 1451, and PHYS 2325 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Introduction to chemical engineering, chemical engineering calculations, unit equations, process stoichiometry, material and energy balances, states of matter, and case studies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
