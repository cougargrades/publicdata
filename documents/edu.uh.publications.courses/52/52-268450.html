COMM 3326 -  Graphics Applications

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: COMM 1307 , COMM 1303 and junior standing.
Description
Application of computer graphics and type principles in print journalism, advertising, art, and public relations.
Repeatability: No

Note: For Communication majors and minors only.
Additional Fee: Y
