PETR 3372 - Petroleum Production Operation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2415 and 3321, and PETR 2313 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Subsurface production fundamentals for producing oil and gas wells with technical emphasis on reservoir inflow, multiphase outflow through the wellbore and surface piping to the separation facility, and artificial life methods.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
