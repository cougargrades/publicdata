AAS 4365 -  Literature of African and the Diaspora

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1301 or AAS 2320 .
Description
Critical survey of literature, such as novels, essays, short fiction, and poetry, written by people of Africa and the diaspora and their culture, ideas, and experiences from the eighteenth century to present.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
