GHL 4360 - Professional Development

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: In order to enroll in this course, you must receive approval for your employment from the instructor prior to enrolling.
Description
Employment in an approved hospitality setting for a cumulative minimum of 700 clock hours in an instructor approved learning situation and participation in a number of career preparation activities.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
