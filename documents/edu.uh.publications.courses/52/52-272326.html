COMM 2326 - Introduction to Sports Broadcasting

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1307 and COMM 1303 or consent of instructor.
Description
Interactive study of 21st century sports broadcasting, including local high school sports live coverage. Students must be able to travel to games.
Repeatability: No

Note: Equipment needed: A digital audio tape recorder or other device for audio recording, microphone optional but recommended (cell phones will work).
Additional Fee: N
