HIST 4388 -  Capstone in Middle East History

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: HIST 2303 or Equivalent, Junior Standing, or Consent of Instructor.
Description
This capstone research course is taught by faculty in the geographic area of Middle East history and explores a variety of topics and time periods reflective of the faculty of record’s teaching specialty, methodological approach, and research interests. In this course, students will produce a subsantial written paper or project that is grounded in historical research using historical sources.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
