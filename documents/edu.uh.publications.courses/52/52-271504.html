WGSS 3350 -  Gender, Society & Feminist Theory

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent. WGSS 2350 or WGSS 2360 or permission of instructor.
Description
This foundational course explores the geneaology of writings essential to gender justice work, past and present. The course employs intersectionality as a framework for analysis of the ways in which race, sexuality, class, nation & dis/abilities inform individuals’ experience of gender.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
