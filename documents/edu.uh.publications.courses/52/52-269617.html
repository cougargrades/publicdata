HIST 4347 -  Century of Revolution: Stuart Britain, 1603-1714 - Capstone

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Students must have completed one of the following: HIST 2303 , HIST 2311 , HIST 2312 , HIST 2322 , HIST 2313 , or HIST 3353 .
Description
This undergraduate seminar centers on political culture, political conflict, and constitutional development in Britain in an era of revolution that both contested and reshaped monarchy.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
