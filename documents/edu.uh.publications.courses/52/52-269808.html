HDFS 4317 -  Introduction to Early Childhood Development

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: HDFS 1300 and HDFS 2314 and prior or concurrent enrollment in HDFS 3350 .
Description
Development from birth to 8 years of age that covers physcial, cognitive, language and literacy, and socio-emotional development, including a practicum.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Lab Fee
