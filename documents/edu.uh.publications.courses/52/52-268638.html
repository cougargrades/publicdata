COSC 4368 - Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: A grade of C- or better in COSC 2320 or COSC 2436 . An overview of main topics in the field of artificial intelligence.
Description
Topics include search techniques, reasoning with logic, planning, decision making, machine learning, and robotics.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
