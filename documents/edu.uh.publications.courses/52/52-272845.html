MUTX 3132 - Music Therapy Practicum II

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MUTX 3131 .
Corequisite: MUTX 3332 .
Description
Students attend Music Therapy group or individual sessions and assist with documentation and data collection, with a focus on early childhood and special education. This contributes to the overall total of 1200 clinical hours required for certification.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
