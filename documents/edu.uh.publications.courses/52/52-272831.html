MATH 4370 - Mathematics for Physicists

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2415 , and MATH 3321 or MATH 3331 .
Description
Vector calculus, tensor analysis, partial differential equations, boundary value problems, series solutions to differential equations, and special functions as applied to junior-senior level physics courses.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students may not receive credit for both MATH 3363 and MATH 4370.
Additional Fee: Yes
