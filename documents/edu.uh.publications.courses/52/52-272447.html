CEA 4424 -  Embedded Computing and Internet of Things

Credit Hours: 4
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: CEA 3338 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Corequisite: CEA 4422 .
Description
Survey of embedded and networked things including sensors, actuators and smart objects, wired and wireless IoT (Internet of Things) network architecture, network protocols, security, data and analytics, industrial and public safety applications of IoT.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
