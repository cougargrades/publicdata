MECE 4340 -  Mechanical Engineering Capstone I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: MECE 3245 , MECE 3338 , MECE 3345 , MECE 3360 , MECE 3381 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MECE 4331 and MECE 4364 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
