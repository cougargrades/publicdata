MECE 5319 -  Introduction to Nanotechnology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3338 , concurrent enrollment in 5119, or consent of instructor; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Field of nanotechnology. Fundamental concepts underlying various nanotechnologies which serves as a leveling course.
