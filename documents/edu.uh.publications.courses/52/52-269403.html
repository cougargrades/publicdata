GERM 3363 -  Themes in German Drama

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Historical, cultural, thematic, and aesthetic evolution of German drama from the Enlightenment to the present.
Repeatability: No

Note: May not apply to foreign language requirement. Taught in English.
Core Category: (50) Core-Creative Arts
Additional Fee: Y
