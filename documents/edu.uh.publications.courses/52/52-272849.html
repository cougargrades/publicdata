MUTX 4133 -  Music Therapy Practicum III

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MUTX 3132 .
Corequisite: MUTX 4333 .
Repeatability: No

Note: Students attend a Music Therapy group and assist with documentation and data collection, with a focus on medical music therapy. This contributes to the overall total of 1200 clinical hours required for certification.
Additional Fee: No
