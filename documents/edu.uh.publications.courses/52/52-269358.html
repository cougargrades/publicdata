GEOL 4355 - Geophysical Field Camp

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: GEOL 1303 , MATH 2414 , and PHYS 2325 .
Description
Cost to be defrayed by student. Field acquisition and interpretation of global positioning satellite (GPS) technology, multicomponent seismic reflection and refraction methods, ground-penetrating radar (GPR), gravity and magentics, well logging, and vertical seismic profiling (VSP).
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
