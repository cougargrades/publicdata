MANA 4382 - Healthcare Strategy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 or equivalent; good academic standing.
Description
Development of strategic thinking based on understanding environmental and internal analysis, sustainable competitive advantage, and organizational strategic leadership in healthcare.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
