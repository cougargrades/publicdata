BTIS 3330 - Fundamentals of Transportation Law and Border Regulations

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0

Description
Knowledge of transportation law, U.S. border regulatory structure, the law making process, and regulatory history and impact.
