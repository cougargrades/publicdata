CHIN 4364 -  Issues in Chinese Language and Linguistics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1301 , CHIN 1501 , and CHIN 1502 .
Description
Issues of Chinese language and linguistics that will enhance appreciation of Chinese culture through analysis of language and linguistics.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taught in English.
Additional Fee: Y
