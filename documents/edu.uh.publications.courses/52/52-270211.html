MATH 4332 -  Introduction to Real Analysis II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 4331 .
Description
Further development and applications of concepts from MATH 4331 . Topics may vary depending instructor’s choice; possibilities include: Fourier series, point-set topology, measure theory, function spaces, or dynamical systems.
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: Y
