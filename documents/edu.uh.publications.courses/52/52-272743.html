Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOL 1306 or BIOL 1307 or BTEC 1322 .

Fundamental programming skills for bioinformatics. Emphasis on DNA and amino acid sequences and microarray profiles.
