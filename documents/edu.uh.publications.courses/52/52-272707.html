ANTH 3391 - Global Ethnographies of Labor

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Any 3000 level (ANTH or WGSS) course or consent of instructor.
Description
This course establishes the centrality of labor in understanding social identities and social change around the world. It emphasizes the cross-cultural meaning of labor. Through ethnographies it locates the effects of larger global processes like development, war, tourism, pandemics, and their changing impact on meaning of labor for peopleâ€™s individual and collective identities.
