MUSI 1116 -  Aural Skills I

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission as music major by audition and credit for, or enrollment in, MUSI 1311 .
Description
Introduction to solfege, melodic and harmonic dictation. Aural skills exercises in diatonic materials, simple meters.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: MUSI 1116
Additional Fee: Y
