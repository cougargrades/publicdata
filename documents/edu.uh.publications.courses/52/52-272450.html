CHEE 5350 -  Advanced Oxidation Processes for Water Treatment

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Corequisite: CHEE 4367 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
This course is designed to provide the fundamentals of advanced oxidation processes (AOPs) currently applied, and under development, for the remediation of water, wastewater and sludge. Topics include UV photolysis and it’s mathematical modeling, and selected industrial applications that include drinking water and textiles.
Repeatability: No
