ENGL 2320 -  Introduction to Digital Humanities

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
Introduction to the Digital Humanities that explores several significant multimodal topics such as data modeling and use; digitization; database design; data-mining and text analysis; mapping and GIS; and web presentation formats.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: Yes
