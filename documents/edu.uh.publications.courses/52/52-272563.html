SOC 3301 - Introduction to Sociological Research

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
The logic of social research and an overview of foundational methods in the social sciences.
Repeatability: No

Note: Priority enrollment given to pre-SOC majors and declared majors or minors in SOC.
Additional Fee: No
