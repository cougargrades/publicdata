WCL 3385 - Cultures and Capitalisms in India and Asia

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1301 .
Description
Exploration of the cultural underpinnings of production, distribution, consumption, and accumulation of resources or pursuit of wealth and prosperity in Asia and India. Will challenge the idea of Asian countries as simply sites for the outsourcing of manufacturing and service industries but rather, explores Asia and India also as centers of innovation and new businesses.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
