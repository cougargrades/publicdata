ARTS 4301 -  Senior Painting Major I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ARTS 3309 - ARTS 3310 - ARTS 3311 and review of portfolio by major faculty.
Corequisite: Concurrent enrollment in ARTS 4300 , ARTS 4301, and ARTS 4302 required.
Description
Development of analytical, conceptual, and critical skills through intensive studio work, critical studies, faculty mentoring, critiques, and visiting artists.
Repeatability: No

Note: ARTS 4300 , ARTS 4301, and ARTS 4302 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree. ARTS 4300-ARTS 4301-ARTS 4302 must be completed prior to ARTS 4303 - ARTS 4304 - ARTS 4305 .
Additional Fee: Y
