QSS 3303 -  Research Quality Assurance

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: QSS 3302 or equivalent.
Description
Assessment, measurement, and interpretation of data. Methods for assuring datasets and databases are accurate, accessible, consistent, reliable, secure, and timely.
