SPAN 3301 -  Spanish Oral Communication for Critical Thinking

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 2312 or by placement exam within a semester prior to enrollment.
Description
For students who have learned Spanish as a foreign language. Heritage learners of Spanish should enroll in SPAN 3307 . Intensive oral practice in groups, class debates and analytical discussions. Exercise critical thinking skills through the analysis of different texts and other forms of cultural production.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
