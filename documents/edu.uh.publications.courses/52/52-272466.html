COMM 3328 -  Introduction to Sports Production

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 2320 and COMM 2328 .
Description
Teaches students how both remote and studio productions are produced, including Esports, streaming internet sports production.
