CEA 4342 - Robotics and Automation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CEA 3421 , CEA 4424 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Coordinate transformations, forward and inverse kinematics, computer vision, motion planning, manipulability, visual servo control, industry applications.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
