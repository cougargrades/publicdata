ARCH 5502 -  Architecture Design Studio-Study Abroad / Traveling

Credit Hours: 5
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: ARCH 2350 , ARCH 2351 , ARCH 3501 .
Description
Design studio: advanced architectural and design ideas. All, or part, of the classes are offered outside of Houston.
