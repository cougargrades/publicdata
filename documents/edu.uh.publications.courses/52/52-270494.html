TLIM 4371 -  Leading Change in the Wrkplace

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Grade of C- or better in TLIM 3340 or equivalent.
Description
For students preparing to assume the role and duties of a leader or supervisor in an organization. Role of leadership and supervisory knowledge and skills in the improvement of productivity and quality.
Repeatability: No
