ARTS 4387 - Site and Sound

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ARTS 1383 or ARTS 3384 .
Description
This course focuses on site-specificity and sound, including documentary sound/field recordings, acoustic ecology, soundscapes, archival sound, and sonic ecologies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
