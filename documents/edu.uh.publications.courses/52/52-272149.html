BIOE 4303 -  Biomaterials

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 3340 and BIOE 3140 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Design and application of materials in hard and soft tissue replacement as well as other applications such as drug delivery systems and tissue engineering.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
