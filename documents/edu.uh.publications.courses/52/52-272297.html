ARCH 3341 -  Contemporary Japanese Architecture

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ARCH 2351 , INDS 2356 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Investigation of the roots of new Japanese architecture, looking beyond the nostalgic argument that Japan’s success is based on its respect towards tradition.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
