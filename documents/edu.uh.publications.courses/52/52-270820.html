QSS 3301 - Regression Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: QSS 2300 or equivalent.
Description
The general linear model and its extensions. Specification and estimation of least squares regression models. Statistical inference and hypothesis testing. Violations of model assumptions and alternative estimators.
