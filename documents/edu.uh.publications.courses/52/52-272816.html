INDS 4211 -  Advanced Topics in Design History

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Total of 3 credits or more from following courses: INDS 3111 , INDS 3211 , INDS 3311 , ARCH 3341 , ARCH 3342 , ARCH 3343 , ARCH 3347 , ARCH 3354 , ARCH 3371 , ARCH 3380 . Two-credit module focused on an advanced selected topic in the history, theory, and criticism of the human- impacted environment. Course Notes: Cross-list as: INAR 4211 and ARCH 4211
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: No
