TLIM 5363 - Fundamental Written Communications

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Approval of department chair.
Description
For graduate students with weak communication skills to manage leveling requirements in their degree plan.
Repeatability: No

Note: For leveling use only, credit cannot be earned toward the degree plan.
Additional Fee: Yes
