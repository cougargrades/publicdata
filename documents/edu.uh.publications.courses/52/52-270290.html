MECE 5367 - Control Systm Analysis & Desgn

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3338 and senior standing in mechanical engineering; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Analysis and design of control systems in mechanical engineering, stability and time response, controller design, state variable approach.
