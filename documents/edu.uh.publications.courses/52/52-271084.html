DRAM 1341 -  Make up for Actors

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Formerly THEA 2323 - Make for Actors
Prerequisite: THEA 1332 and DRAM 1351 .
Description
Introduction of basic stage makeup materials, methods, and techniques with an emphasis on applications for theatre and film.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: DRAM 1341
Additional Fee: Y
