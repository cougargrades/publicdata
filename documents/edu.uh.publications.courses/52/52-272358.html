ELET 4354 -  Biomedical Image Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ELET 2300 or equivalent, ELET 3301 .
Description
Digital image processing from an operational perspective. Aspects of image acquisition, digitization, enhancement, restoration and segmentation in practical applications.
