NURS 4218 - Emergency Nursing

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: NURS 3631 & NURS 3633 or NURS 3312 & NURS 3313
Description
Focus on emergency/trauma care of patients. Introduction to concepts and skills required in Emergency Department settings. Clinical work in assigned facility.
Note: Clinical 32 hours in assigned facility.
