CIVE 4381 -  Satellite-based Geomatics and Geosensing

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of two of the following courses: CIVE 3331 , CIVE 3332 , CIVE 3337 , CIVE 3339 , and CIVE 3434 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Principles of global navigation satellite system; introduction to satellite remote sensing; reference system; map projection; online geospatial processing services.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
