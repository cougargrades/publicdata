ECE 3457 - Digital Electronics

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: ECE 3355 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Analysis of discrete and integrated digital electronic devices and components and their use in the design and implementation of digital circuits.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
