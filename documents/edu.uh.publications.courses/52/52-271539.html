GEOL 4367 -  Geochemical Reaction Modeling

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Declared major in the Department of Earth and Atmospheric Sciences; CHEM 1112 , CHEM 1312 , and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 2414 , and GEOL 3370 or GEOL 3340 .
Description
Theory and application of geochemical reaction modeling. Approaches to modeling aqueous processes and their applications in diverse geological and environmental systems.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
