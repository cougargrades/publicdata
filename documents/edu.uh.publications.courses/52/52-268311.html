CHEM 2323 -  Organic Chemistry I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 1312 .
Description
Chemistry of the compounds of carbon with emphasis on energies and mechanism of reactions, synthesis, and the structure of organic molecules.
Repeatability: No

Note: May not apply toward degree until CHEM 2123 is successfully completed.
TCCNS Equivalent: CHEM 2323
Additional Fee: Y
