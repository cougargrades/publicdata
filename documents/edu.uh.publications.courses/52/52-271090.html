THEA 2342 - Dramatic Structures & Genres

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: THEA 1332 , DRAM 1352 , ENGL 1301 , completion of or/ concurrent enrollment in ENGL 1302 , and instructor consent.
Description
Survey of the fundamental aspects of dramatic structures and theatrical genres. Critical approaches for a better understanding of how given plays accomplish their theatrical goals.
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: Y
