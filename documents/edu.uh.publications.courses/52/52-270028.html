KIN 1352 -  Foundations of Kinesiology, Health, and Fitness

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly KIN 1252 Foundations of Kinesiological Studies
Prerequisite: none
Description
Introductory information on, and  historical backgrounds of the fields of kinesiology, health, and fitness. Students will assess their own health and fitness levels.
