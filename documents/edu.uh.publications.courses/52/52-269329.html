GEOL 3370 -  Mineralogy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Declared EAS majors or permission from EAS undergraduate faculty advisor and CHEM 1111 , CHEM 1311 , GEOL 1303 , and GEOL 1103 .
Description
Identification and description of minerals, including their crystallographic, physical, chemical, and structural properties.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
