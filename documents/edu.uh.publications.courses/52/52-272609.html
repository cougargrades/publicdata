ARTS 3300 - Advanced Drawing

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ARTS 1316 or ARTS 1317 .
Description
Refinement of basic skills and further exploration of drawing as an expressive and experimental image-making method through expanded and non-traditional media and approaches.
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: No
