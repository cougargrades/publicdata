INDE 4337 - Human Factors & Ergonomics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INDE 4331 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Measurement and evaluation of human-machine systems; techniques and procedures for developing and applying principles of human factors engineering to system design.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
