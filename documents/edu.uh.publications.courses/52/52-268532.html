COMD 1333 - Introduction to Communication Sciences and Disorders

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Survey course describing the professions of speech-language pathology, audiology, and speech, language and hearing sciences. Overview of The Big Nine disorder areas and career opportunities in service delivery and research.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
