MUSI 2116 - Aural Skills III

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MUSI 1117 and passing grade on Aural Skills Proficiency Exam I; credit for or concurrent enrollment in MUSI 2210 .
Description
Tenor clef; secondary dominants; elementary modulation; more advanced rhythmic problems, including borrowed divisions.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: MUSI 2116
Additional Fee: Y
