ARTS 4330 -  Senior Graphic Design Major I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ARTS 3335 - ARTS 3336 - ARTS 3337 and review of portfolio by major faculty.
Corequisite: Concurrent enrollment in ARTS 4330, ARTS 4331 , and ARTS 4332 required.
Description
Advanced studies in graphic design, including design theory and critical thinking, complex problem solving, and concept development skills, identify and branding, infographics, environmental graphic design and user interface solutions.
Repeatability: Yes

Note: ARTS 4330, ARTS 4331 , and ARTS 4332 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree. Senior graphic design major courses ARTS 4330-ARTS 4331-ARTS 4332 and ARTS 4333 - ARTS 4334 - ARTS 4335 must be taken in consecutive semesters.
Additional Fee: Y
