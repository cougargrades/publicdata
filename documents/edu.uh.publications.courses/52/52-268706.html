CUIN 3310 - Foundations of Bilingual Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: six semester hours of advanced Spanish or equivalent.
Description
Rationale and history of bilingual programs for limited or non-English speaking students. Taught in Spanish.
