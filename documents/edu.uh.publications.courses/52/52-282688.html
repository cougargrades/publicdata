INTN 2305 - American Sign Language: Advanced 1

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: SGNL 1302
Description
Continuation of SGNL 1302 - for INTN majors only; understanding and expression of expanded and specialized lexicon, the use of classifiers and rules for categorization.
Note: Language lab access and community involvement required in addition to class meetings.
