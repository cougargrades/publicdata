SOC 3312 -  Sociology of Deviance

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 .
Description
An examination of the social and structural causes of behavior that violate the norm and how society responds to the people it defines as criminal, crazy, strange, immoral, and sick.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
