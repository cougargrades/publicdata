MANA 4372 - Healthcare Leadership

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 or equivalent; good academic standing.
Description
Development of the skills needed to elicit commitment and productivity from people and teams in the rapidly changing healthcare environment.
