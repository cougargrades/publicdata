MECE 2361 -  Introduction to Mechanical Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100 .
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in ENGI 2304 and in ENGR 1304 or equivalent drafting experience; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Introduction to engineering design. Individual and group projects.
