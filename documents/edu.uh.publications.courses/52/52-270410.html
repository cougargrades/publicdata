MUSI 3215 -  Introduction To Large Forms

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 2214 and a passing grade on Theory Proficiency Exam IV and Aural Skills Proficiency Exam II.
Description
Introduction to the study of larger musical forms of the eighteenth through twentieth centuries; introduction to the analysis of contrapuntal textures.
