GEOL 4342 -  Deep Learning for Big Data in Environmental Science

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: GEOL 1303 or GEOL 1302 or GEOL 1347 or GEOL 3378 ; MATH 2318 or COSC 1336 ; any upper division GEOL course.
Description
Principles of climate systems and using geological materials as a tool to interpret climate and environment throughout history.
