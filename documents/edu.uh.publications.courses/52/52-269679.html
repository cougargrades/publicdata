GHL 2368 - Room and Housekeeping Mgt

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GHL 1335 (formerly HRMA 1335), and credit for or concurrent enrollment in GHL 1367 (formerly HRMA 1367).
Description
Operation of a rooms department of a typical full-service or selective service lodging facility. Management responsibilities together with operational techniques.
Repeatability: No

Typically Offered: Fall, Spring, & Summer

Additional Fee: Yes
