TLIM 3372 -  Communicatn in Sci,Engr & Tech

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: three hours in communication course with a grade of C or higher; successful completion of computer application course (i.e. MS Word).
Description
Analysis of issues and trends in science, engineering, and technology to develop effective communication in the global workforce. Focus on oral and written communications, critical thinking, and research skills using computer-driven technologies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
