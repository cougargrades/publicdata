THEA 3372 -  Digital Design Techniques

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: THEA 2334 , DRAM 1330 , DRAM 2331 , THEA 2373 , and consent of instructor.
Description
Study and use of software, tools, and techniques to create, capture, and manipulate images for scenic, lighting, and costume designers.
