ANTH 3381 - Hindu Religion and Identity: A Global Movement

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Cross-Listed As: RELS 3381

Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
This course focuses on how local and global cultures take shape within a complex weave of exchanges. Diversity within the Hindu religion will be explored.
Repeatability: No
