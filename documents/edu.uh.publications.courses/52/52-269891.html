INDS 4501 -  Industrial Design Studio VIII

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: INDS 4500 .
Description
Develop senior project with an emphasis on systematic and human-centered design. Student’s design process and final design are presented at the juried graduation exhibition.
Additional Fee: Y
