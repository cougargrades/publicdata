MARK 4377 -  Sales for Social Impact

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: MARK 3337 and approval of program coordinator.
Description
Selling and sales force management in the context of social enterprises.
Note: This course is open to non-business students in the Program for Excellence in Selling.
