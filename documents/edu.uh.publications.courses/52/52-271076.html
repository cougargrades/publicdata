DRAM 1351 -  Acting I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Acceptance to THEA major by audition/portfolio review, concurrent enrollment in THEA 1332 , and consent of instructor.
