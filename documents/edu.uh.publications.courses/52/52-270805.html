PSYC 4371 -  Organizational Psychology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Theory and research on work motivation, leadership, and related aspects of group behavior in organizations.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
