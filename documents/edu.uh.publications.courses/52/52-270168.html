MATH 1324 -  Finite Math with Applications

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Systems of linear equations, introduction to linear programming, mathematics of finance, topics in probability and statistics.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students with prior credit for MATH 2318 will not receive credit for MATH 1324. May not apply to course or GPA requirements for a major or minor in natural sciences and mathematics.
Core Category: (90) Core-Math/Reasoning, Component Area Option
(20) Core-Mathematics
TCCNS Equivalent: MATH 1324
Additional Fee: Y
