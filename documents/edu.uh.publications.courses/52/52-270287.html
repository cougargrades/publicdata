MECE 5363 -  Fluid Mechanics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3363 and senior standing in Mechanical Engineering; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Flow in piping systems, boundary layers and bluff body flows, inviscid flow, and turbomachinery.
