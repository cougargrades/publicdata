BIOE 2201 -  BioCircuit Analysis

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100 , ENGI 1331 , ENGL 1302 or equivalent, PHYS 2325 with a grade of C- or better.
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 2415 , MATH 3321 (or MATH 2318 and MATH 3331 ), PHYS 2326 .
Description
Analysis of resistive circuits, including node voltage and mesh current methods, Thevenin and Norton equivalent circuits, and potential applications in biomedical engineering. Fundamental concepts in electrical and bio equivalent circuit analysis, and applications in Biomedical Engineering.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
