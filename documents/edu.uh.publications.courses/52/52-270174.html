MATH 2450 - Accelerated Calculus I

Credit Hours: 4
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: At least one semester of advanced placement high school calculus and placement out of MATH 2312 .
Description
The first course in an accelerated calculus sequence. Includes topics normally covered in MATH 2413 and MATH 2414 .
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be received for both MATH 2450 and MATH 2413 .
Core Category: (90) Core-Math/Reasoning, Component Area Option, (20) Core-Mathematics
Additional Fee: Yes
