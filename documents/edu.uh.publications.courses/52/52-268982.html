ELET 3307 - Electrical Machines & Controls

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in ELET 2301 and MATH 2413 or consent of instructor.
