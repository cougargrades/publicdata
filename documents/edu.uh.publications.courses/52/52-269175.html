ENTR 4330 - Entrepreneurial Costs and Budgets

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENTR 3310 or ENTR 3311 .
Description
Cost research and relationships between income, balance sheets, cash flow, capital budgets, and operating budget statements.
