INTN 4346 -  ASL Transliteration & Educational Interpreting

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INTN major and INTN 3434 or permission of the instructor.
Description
Interpreting in classroom settings. The interpreter’s role with educators, interpreting issues related to school-age students, and federal regulations related to interpreting.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
