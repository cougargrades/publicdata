ITAL 1502 - Elementary Italian II

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: ITAL 1501 or equivalent.
Description
Credit for ITAL 1502 may not be applied to a degree by students who have credit for ITAL 1302 /ITAL 1102 . Understanding, speaking, reading, and writing Italian. Emphasis on oral expression and listening comprehension.
TCCNS Equivalent: ITAL 1512
