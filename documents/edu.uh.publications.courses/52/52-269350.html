GEOL 4334 - Environmental Data Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: GEOL 1302 or GEOL 1347 , and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 2415 .
Description
Physical and mathematical basis of environmental data analysis. Topics include basic concepts of statistics, regression, filtering, and principal component analysis, etc. Course Notes: Credit cannot be applied for GEOL 4334 and GEOL 6328.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
