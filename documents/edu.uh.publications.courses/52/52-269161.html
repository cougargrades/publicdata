ENGL 4376 -  Robin Hood in Culture

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Adaptations of the Robin Hood narratives in medieval through contemporary texts and various media, including film. Emphasizes how the developing legend reflects changing cultural issues.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
