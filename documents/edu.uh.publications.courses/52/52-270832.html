RELS 2340 - Lived Hindu Religion

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
The Hindu religion is one of the oldest and the newest at the same time. This course uses the lens of lived religions. A revolving door approach of present-past-present will be applied to get a better understanding of the Hindu way of being.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: Yes
