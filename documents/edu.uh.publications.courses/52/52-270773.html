PSYC 3331 -  Psychology of Gender

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Empirically based gender differences as they impact individual and social behaviors, roles and public policy.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
