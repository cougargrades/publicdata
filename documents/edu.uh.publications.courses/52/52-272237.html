BIOL 4370 -  Ecology and Development

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: BIOL 3301 ; Recommended: BIOL 3306 .
Description
An exploration of the impact of the environmental ecology on adaptation and evolution of organisms through phenotypic plasticity, genetic changes in gene regulation, epigenetics, and variations in developmental pathways.
