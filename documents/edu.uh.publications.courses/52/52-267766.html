INTN 4335 - Advanced Interpreting Techniques and Skills in ASL/English

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INTN 3433 and INTN 3434 .
Description
Builds on the ASL interpreting skills acquired in INTN 3433 and INTN 3434. Increasing task demands and complex communication contexts are emphasized.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
