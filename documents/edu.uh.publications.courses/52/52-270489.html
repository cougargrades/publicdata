TLIM 4341 - Production & Service Operation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Grade of C- or better in: TLIM 3340 and University approved statistics ( TMTH 3360 or MATH 1342 or equivalent).
Description
Planning and control of production and service operations. Product and service design, design of work systems, forecasting, scheduling, capacity planning, and inventory control
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
