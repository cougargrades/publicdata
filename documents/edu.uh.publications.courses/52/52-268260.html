CHEE 4367 - Chemical Reaction Engineering

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEE 3369 , CHEM 2325 and CHEE 3466 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Chemical reaction kinetics, mechanisms, and reactor design in static and flow systems; introduction to heterogeneous catalytic reactions in flow systems.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
