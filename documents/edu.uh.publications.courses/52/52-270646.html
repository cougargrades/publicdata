PHYS 4350 - Computational Physics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 3315 , MATH 3331 or MATH 3321 , and COSC 1336 .
Description
Simulation of classical and quantum mechanical problems on digital computers using numerical and modern programming techniques.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
