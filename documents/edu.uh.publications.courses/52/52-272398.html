NURS 4217 -  Maternal, Newborn, and Women’s Health Clinical

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 90
Prerequisite: NURS 3310 , NURS 3311 , NURS 3315 , NURS 3312 , NURS 3313 , NURS 3314 .
Corequisite: NURS 4215 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in: NURS 4316 , NURS 4417 .
Description
This course provides clinical practice opportunities in infant and childbearing settings to enable the student to acquire intermediate nursing care skills.
Additional Fee: N
