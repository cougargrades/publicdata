GEOL 3344 -  Paleoclimatology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: GEOL 1303 , GEOL 1103 ; CHEM 1312 ; MATH 2312 or MATH 2413 .
Description
Principles of climate systems and using geological materials as a tool to interpret climate and environment throughout history.
