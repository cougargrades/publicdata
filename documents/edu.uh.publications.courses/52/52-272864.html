POLS 3393 - Model United Nations

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2305 and GOVT 2306 .
Description
This course teaches international politics and diplomacy through simulations of the United Nations. Students will learn about the workings of the UN, what topics it covers, and how states behave within the UN.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
