POLS 4350 - Political Economy of War

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 , or Consent of Instructor.
Description
Examines the intersection of economic and security issues in international relations, specifically how economics affects war and war economics.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
