PHYS 1302 -  College Physics II (lecture)

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 1301 .
Description
Second semester of a two part non-calculus-based course covering thermodynamics, electricity, magnetism, electromagnetic waves, optics, and modern physics.
Repeatability: No

Note: Primarily for majors other than physics and engineering. Credit may not be applied toward a degree for both PHYS 1302 and PHYS 2326.
Core Category: (30) Core-Life & Physical Sciences
TCCNS Equivalent: PHYS 1302
Additional Fee: Y
