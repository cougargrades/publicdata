ITAL 2601 -  Accelerated Italian Second Year

Credit Hours: 6
Lecture Contact Hours: 6 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ITAL 1501 , ITAL 1502 or ITAL 1601 .
Description
Second Year Italian Language taught in one semester.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taken after ITAL 1501 and ITAL 1502 or ITAL 1601, it fulfills language requirement. Students can take ITAL 2601 after taking ITAL 2311 . After completing ITAL 2601, students cannot take ITAL 2312 .
Additional Fee: N
