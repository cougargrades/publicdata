PHIL 3357 - Punishment

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: three semester hours in philosophy or consent of instructor.
Description
This course examines a range of philosophical theories of punishment, paying close attention to what these theories presume about human agency and responsibility.
