PSYC 4354 - Brain and Behavior

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), PSYC 2330 or consent of instructor.
Description
Relationship between the structure and function of the human central nervous system and the behavior of both normal and impaired individuals.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
