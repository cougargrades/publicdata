INDE 3310 - Statistical Process Quality Control and Improvement

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INDE 2333 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Methods and philosophy of statistical process control; Control charts for variables and attributes; CUSUM and EWNA control charts; Process and measurement system capability analysis; Lot-by-lot acceptance sampling for attributes; Continuous quality improvement tools, and six-sigma.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
