SPAN 2312 - Intermediate Spanish II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of SPAN 2311 with a minimum grade of C- in twelve months immediately prior to enrollment or by placement exam immediately prior to enrollment.
Description
Heritage speakers of Spanish not eligible to enroll. Cultural and literary readings, composition, and grammar review.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: SPAN 2312
Additional Fee: Y
