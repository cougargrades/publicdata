PSYC 4363 -  Abnormal Child Psych

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 , PSYC 2305 , PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Introduction to childhood psychopathology, in the context of normal child development, with emphasis on psychological factors responsible for deviations.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
