NURS 4417 -  Medical Surgical Clinical

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 12
Prerequisite: NURS 3310 , NURS 3311 , NURS 3315 , NURS 3312 , NURS 3313 , NURS 3314 , NURS 4521 .

Description
This course provides clinical practice opportunities in acute care settings to enable the student to acquire intermediate nursing care skills for adult patients.
