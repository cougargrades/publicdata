POLS 3368 -  Race,Gender & Ethnic Politics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalents, or consent of instructor.
Description
Examines the roles, strategies and different levels of success minority groups and women have had in the American political system. (American Politics category.)
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
