ENGI 1101 -  Engineering Success

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: None.
Corequisite: MATH 2312 .
Description
A non-degree applicable first year engineering success course providing in-class and online self-assessment activities covering learning/critical thinking /study skills, motivation, accountability, and strategic planning.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
