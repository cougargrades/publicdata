RELS 2330 -  Judaism

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly RELS 3310
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Judaism from the post-biblical era to the present, including ancient, medieval, and rabbinic literature, philosophy, and mysticism; Jewish responses to modernity and the Holocaust; American and Israeli Judaism.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (40) Core-Language, Philosophy & Culture
Additional Fee: Y
