GEOL 3382 - Atmospheric Chemistry

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: GEOL 1302 or GEOL 1347 , CHEM 1311 , and credit for or concurrent enrollment in CHEM 1312 .
Corequisite: CHEM 1312 .
Description
Introduction to the principles of atmospheric chemistry and their relationship to global change.
Repeatability: No

Note: Basic instrumentation will be covered in the lab portion.
Additional Fee: Y
