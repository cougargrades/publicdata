MECE 2334 -  Thermodynamics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331 , CHEM 1112 , CHEM 1312 , MATH 2415 , and PHYS 2326 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Fundamental concepts of heat and work, simple substances, energy analysis, first and second laws, thermodynamics of state and power cycles.
Repeatability: No
