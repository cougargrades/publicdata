WGSS 3321 -  Gender in Transnational Perspective

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent. WGSS 2350 or WGSS 2360 , or by permission of instructor.
Description
This course explores the impact of globatization on the gendered experiences of individuals in varied contexts around the world, with particular focus on women of color and sexual minorities.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
