RELS 2310 -  Introduction to the Hebrew Bible/Old Testament

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Bible as a primary document of Western culture, basic to the understanding of the western philosophical, literary, cultural, and scientific tradition. Focus on ideas developed in Hebrew Bible (Old Testament) and their literary, philosophical and political impact.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (40) Core-Language, Philosophy & Culture
Additional Fee: Yes
