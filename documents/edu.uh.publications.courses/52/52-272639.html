GEOL 4371 -  Electromagnetic Methods for Energy, Groundwater, and Environmental Applications

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: MATH 2415 and PHYS 2326 .
Description
Basic concepts and theory in applied electromagnetics; data acquisition systems; applications to hydrology, geohazards, and natural resources; interpretation of electromagnetic data.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
