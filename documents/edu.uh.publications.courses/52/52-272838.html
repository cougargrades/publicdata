MUTX 2102 -  Music Styles and Skills II

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MUTX 2101 .
Description
Piano and guitar skills for music therapists and music educators. Foundations for playing and harmonizing Blues, Jazz, Latin, and Gospel music on both guitar and piano.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
