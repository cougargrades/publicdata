AAS 3377 - Black Feminist Thought

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: AAS 2320 .
Description
Black Feminist Thought seeks to exsplain how Black women are defined in a variety of cultural contexts, as well as examine how race, gender and sexuality are central to the analysis of Black Feminist Thought. Students will become fluent in the grammar of intersectionality, and increase their understandings of the social construct of racialized societies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
