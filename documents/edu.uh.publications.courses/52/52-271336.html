ARCH 3327 - Technology 3

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ARCH 2328 and PHYS 1301 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in ARCH 3500 .
Description
Intermediate level investigation and analysis of structural systems and their materials, climate/human environment responsive systems (mechanical, electrical and plumbing), facility program and code implications.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
