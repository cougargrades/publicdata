ELED 3320 -  Survey of Literature for Children and Adolescents

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: sophomore standing and admission to Teacher Education.
Description
Reading and evaluation of books, guidance of reading interests, and techniques for promoting lifelong reading among elementary and adolescent students.
