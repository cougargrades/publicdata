ECE 2202 - Circuit Analysis II

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ECE 2201 and PHYS 2126 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Analysis of electric circuits, including inductors, capacitors, and first order circuits; sinusoidal analysis.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
