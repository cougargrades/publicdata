ENGL 2321 - Writing, Technology, and Social Media

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
This course explores the intersection of writing, technology, and social media in a 21st century context by examining media sources and creating multimodal texts.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: Yes
