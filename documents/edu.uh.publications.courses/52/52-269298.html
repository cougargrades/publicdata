GEOL 1303 -  Physical Geology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Credit for, placement out of, or concurrent enrollment in MATH 1314 or MATH 1324 or MATH 1332 (formerly MATH 1310, or MATH 1313, or MATH 1311).
Corequisite: MATH 1314 or MATH 1324 or MATH 1332 (formerly MATH 1310, or MATH 1313, or MATH 1311).
Description
Principles of geology; emphasis on surface and internal processes of the earth.
