LST 4392 -  Internship in Public Law

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2305 and GOVT 2306 or equivalent.
Description
This internship is intended for students interested in going to Law School or into law-related fields. The internship will provide students with hands-on experience in organizations that focus on public interest law, which seeks to help the public on a wide range of issues related to rights, health and welfare, and the representation of marginalized groups or interests. Interns will be placed in governmental and non-governmental organizations or specialized law firms.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
