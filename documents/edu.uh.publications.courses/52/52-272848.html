MUTX 3360 -  Psychology of Music

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUTX 2301 and acceptance into Music Therapy major.
Description
Psychological foundations of music, including cognition, perception, emotional meaning; musical development and learning; and physiologic responses to music.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
