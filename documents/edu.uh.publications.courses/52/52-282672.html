POLS 3377 - Money in American Politics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2305 and GOVT 2306 or equivalent.
Description
An evaluation of the role money plays in contemporary American politics. Specific topics include campaign finance, lobbying, corporate and union politics, and wealth inequality.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
