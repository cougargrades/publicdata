INDE 3333 -  Engineering Economy I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INDE 2333 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Description
Time value of money, depreciation, after tax evaluation, decisions under uncertainty and risk. Economic evaluation of engineering alternatives and proposals.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
