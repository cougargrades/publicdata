MATH 4365 - Numerical Methods for Differential Equations

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3331 , or equivalent, and three additional hours of 3000-4000 level Mathematics.
Description
Numerical differentiation and integration, multi-step and Runge-Kutta methods for ODEs, finite difference and finite element methods for PDEs, iterative methods for linear algebraic systems and eigenvalue computation.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
