PHIL 3363 - Philosophy of Film

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Three semester hours in philosophy.
Description
Students should acquire from this course a solid understanding of the principal issues in contemporary analytic philosophy of film as well as how these issues intersect with a variety of disciplines outside of philosophy (e.g., film studies, art history, psychology and cognitive science).
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
