ITAL 3333 - Women of the Italian Renaissance

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
Works of women writers and artists of Early Modern Italy are explored in conjunction with current scholarship on Renaissance and gender studies.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taught in English.
Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: No
