BIOL 4340 - Research Methods

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: CUIN 3351 and one of CHEM 1111 , CHEM 1117, BIOL 1106 , BIOL 1107 ; PHYS 1101 , PHYS 2125 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
Students perform four independent inquiries, combing skills from mathematics and science to solve research problems.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit may not be received for BIOL 4340 and CHEM 4340 or PHYS 4340 .
Additional Fee: Y
