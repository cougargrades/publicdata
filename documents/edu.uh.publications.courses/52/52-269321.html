GEOL 3338 -  Environmental Hydrogeology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Declared major in the Department of Earth and Atmospheric Sciences; PHYS 2325 , MATH 2413 , and credit for or concurrent enrollment in GEOL 3370 or GEOL 3340 .
Description
Principles of groundwater and surface water flow and interaction with the environment: water quality and pollution.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
