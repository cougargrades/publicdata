CHIN 1209 - Chinese Calligraphy

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Techniques of writing Chinese characters with a brush-pen. Integrates the Chinese language and culture by studying the formation of characters, and basic styles of Chinese calligraphy.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taught in English.
Additional Fee: Y
