MECT 4364 -  Smart Manufacturing Systems Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: MECT 3360 .
Description
Design principles and applications of complex automated manufacturing & materials handling systems, including IoT (Internet of Things) and data analytics for smart manufacturing.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
