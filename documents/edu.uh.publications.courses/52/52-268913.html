ECE 3355 - Electronics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECE 2202 ; all prerequisites must be completed with C- or higher.
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in ECE 3337 and ECE 3155 .
Description
Signal and amplifier concepts; operational amplifiers; diodes and nonlinear circuits; bipolar junction transistors; biasing, small and large signal analysis; transistor amplifiers.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
