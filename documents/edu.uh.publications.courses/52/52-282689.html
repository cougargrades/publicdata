INTN 2306 - American Sign Language: Advanced 2

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: INTN 2305
Description
Continuation of INTN 2305 - for INTN majors only; narrative and expository genre explained and enhanced descriptive ability explored.
Note: Language lab access and community involvement required in addition to class meetings.
