MAS 3344 -  Research Methods in Mexican American and Latino/a Studies

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Training in different research methodologies used to explore the Mexican American/Latina/o population and its experiences. Students will conduct their own research.
Repeatability: The course may be repeated twice when the research topics change.

Additional Fee: No
