MUED 4240 -  Teaching Private Music Lessons to Secondary Students

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Admission as instrumental music major by audition.
Description
Introduces logistical and general pedagogical topics for music students who are preparing to teach private lessons to middle school and high school students.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
