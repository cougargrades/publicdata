AAS 4310 - The Black Middle Class

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: AAS 2320 .
Description
The Black Middle Class examines the myraid sociocultural and political factors that contributed to the emergence of the Black middle class and the impact of growing class stratification within the Black community.
