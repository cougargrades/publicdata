GEOL 3345 -  Structural Geology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GEOL 3372 or GEOL 3325 [Inactive], GEOL 3340 , MATH 2413 , PHYS 1301 or PHYS 2325 , and credit for or concurrent enrollment in GEOL 3145 .
Corequisite: GEOL 3145 .
Description
Properties of earth minerals and their behavior in stress fields; description, classification, and interpretation of geologic structures.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
