SCM 4311 -  Project Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: SCM 3301 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Management of large-scale and complex one-time ventures. Topics include organizational design, work breakdown structure, network analysis, cost and schedule control, and software application.
