INDI 3383 - India and South Asia in Diaspora

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 .
Description
Explores the experiences and imagined worlds of South Asians who have traveled to various parts of the world at different historical moments beginning with colonial labor migrations to the present. Examines different forms of labor, and Third World and First World nationalisms.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
