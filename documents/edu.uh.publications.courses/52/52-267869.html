ARCH 2327 -  Technology I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: MATH 2312 and ARCH 1501 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in ARCH 2500 .
Description
Introductory survey of site, structural, and environmental systems (including mechanical, electrical and plumbing), including site visits, simulations and constructing analytical models and drawings.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
