SOC 3342 -  Sociology of Work

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
Modern industrialism as social behavior; work groups as social systems; assumption and values with regard to the meaning and place of work in American society.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
