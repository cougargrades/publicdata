SOC 3337 - Women and Crime

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
The role of women within the criminal justice system; women as victims and offenders of crime; gender differences in criminal justice careers; the impact of the criminal justice system on women.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
