BIOL 6330 - Molecular Basis of Infectious Diseases

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None, but BCHS 3304 or equivalent is strongly recommended.

Molecular mechanisms of pathogenesis by selected bacterial and viral pathogens, with emphasis on basic concepts in microbial physiology and biochemistry.
