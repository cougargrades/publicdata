PHAR 4400 - Cellular Life Sciences I

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: First year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

To describe the basic principles of cellular and biochemical basis of metabolism of nutrients including proteins, carbohydrates, lipids, and nucleic acids and their relationship with disease states and drug therapy.
