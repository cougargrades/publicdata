COSC 6391 - Biomedical Image Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: consent of instructor.

Principles and computational methods for the analysis of biomedical images. Topics include segmentation, classification, registration and validation.
