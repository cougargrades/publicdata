OPTO 5282 - Community Health Optometry

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
This course is designed to acquaint the student with the organization of the health care delivery system and to provide the underpinnings of the profession including its history and socioeconomic, ethical, and legal elements. It will include epidemiology and biostatistics as they apply to optometry.
