PHOP 7276 - MATLAB Programming for Vision Science

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: An introductory programming course in C++ or another language that teaches basic programming concepts and skills.

Corequisite: Corequisite(s): laboratory research practicum in mentorâ€™s lab.

This course teaches MATLAB programming concepts and skills for use in research in vision science. The course has a didactic portion for learning to use MATLAB, and a project portion in which the student works with the help of the instructor(s) to develop a program for his or her own research project to acquire or analyze data, or both.
