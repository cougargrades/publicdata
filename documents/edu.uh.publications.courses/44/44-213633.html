WGSS 4360 - Capstone Internship Course

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent. WGSS 3350 or WGSS 3360 or permission of the instructor. Student should be a registered WGSS major and have completed all or most of their other required coursework for the major.
Description
