AAMS 2300 - Introduction to Asian American Studies

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly CUST 2300.
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Course Description: Introduces students to interdisciplinary analyses of Asian peoples as a “race” and multi-ethnicities in the US. Examines some of the dominant historical experiences and issues that shape Asian American. Explores the diversity and complexity of Asian American histories and experiences within the context of US race and ethnic relations.
